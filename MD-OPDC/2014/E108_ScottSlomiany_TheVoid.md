### Visual Description
The page is presented in a stark, high-contrast black-and-white style. It functions as a "relationship map"—a web of interconnected text boxes and circular character portraits. The top-left features the title "VOID." The center of the page is a complex flowchart of boxes linked by arrows representing personal relationships. The right-hand column contains two vertical lists of secrets, and the bottom section provides a narrative introduction and instructions for game mechanics involving "secret" and "infected" cards that the GM places on the map.
