### Transcription of Text

**Seas, Scales, and Skulls**
*By John Warner*
Tasked with finding a merchant vessel lost near the mainland’s high bluffs, adventurers discover that lizardfolk pirates have enslaved the crew. The scaly brigands seek to break into a legendary lizardfolk hero’s tomb to seize his magical weapons, rally their kin, and take revenge on the human city for winning the war that forced them from their lands.

**Legend**
(1) Dock and deconstructed merchant ship. Ambush: Skirmishers from behind ship, Archers shoot from rock.
(2) Sleeping area. Two fire pits and scattered bedrolls. Locked chest has key to all cages, mundane gear, and coins.
(3) Carved hallway leads to (4), stairs lead to (5). Two Skirmishers ahead go to (4), talking about tomb guards.
(4) Water room. Boxes provide cover for archers. Skirmishers and Archers present. Cage on far wall has some Crewmembers. Saltwater Crocodiles lurk in water. Hidden, underwater path to (6); the crew knows it is there.
(5) Bridge over gorge. Several Archers on opposite side.
(6) Shaman’s room. Chest contains his writings, a key to (11), and treasure (magic items). Chest has poison trap.
(7) Basking room lit by small holes above. Coffin has carvings of snakes pointed at suns and away from moons. Turn the four snakes on the pillars to match them to open cover. Magic weapon inside. Snakes bask near tomb.
(8) Preparation room. Mage guards First Mate in cage. The latter gives hints and healing (the pirates liked her singing).
(9) Each pillar here has several dart traps. Faint, safe path carved into debris-strewn floor. Guardian Constrictors.
(10) Both doors to this web-covered room are stuck and require force to open. Giant Spiders. Hidden way to (13).
(11) Storeroom barred by locked door from ship. It holds crank for bridge, treasure chest, and Skeleton Guards.
(12) Swinging bridge over chasm needs crank from (11) to raise it. Other side has crank as a hint. Bat Swarm.
(13) Locked tomb room. Many Skeleton Guards. Treasure.
(14) Open, looted tomb. Wandering Skeleton Guards.
(15) Sacrifice room; fire trap on door. Raised walkway with pillars around edge. Shaman, Archers, Skirmishers. Shaman pushes Captain into center pit to open (16).
(16) Opens a while after trigger. Angry Hero’s Specter and Skeleton Guards attack everyone. Treasure in tomb.

**Monsters:**
*   **Shaman:** Lizardfolk. A pirate captain, powerful spellcaster, and descendant of the entombed hero. Seeks revenge for the war he started—and lost.
*   **Mage:** Lizardfolk. She uses fire and summon spells.
*   **Skirmisher:** Lizardfolk. Uses spears and shields.
*   **Archer:** Lizardfolk. Wields a bow. Is good at sniping.
*   **Skeleton Guard:** Undead lizardfolk armed with an axe or a bow. Attacks all intruders, even the pirates.
*   **Saltwater Crocodile:** A large, hungry, trained lizard.
*   **Snakes:** Poisonous; will attack and flee if disturbed.
*   **Giant Spider:** Poisonous; likes to ambush from webs.
*   **Guardian Constrictor:** Giant, fleshy, undead snake.
*   **Bat Swarm:** Hungry for blood. Attacks passersby.
*   **Crewmember:** Human. Has info on recent events.
*   **First Mate:** Human. Has info on tomb and shaman. Kept alive by her magic healing. Offers such aid.
*   **Captain:** Human. As the leader, is the ritual sacrifice.
*   **Hero’s Specter:** Enraged wraith of a lizardfolk hero.

### Map Description
The map represents a dungeon carved into a rocky cliffside, oriented with a central, jagged gorge running vertically through the middle.

*   **Entrance (Right Side):** The dungeon begins at a dock (1) at the bottom right. A ship is being broken down here. To the left of the dock is a sleeping area (2). A hallway (3) leads north into a large water-filled chamber (4). This chamber connects via an underwater passage to the upper central section (6).
*   **Central Gorge:** A bridge (5) spans a deep, dark gorge that splits the map vertically. 
*   **The Northern/Central Section:** Above the water room and across the gorge is the Shaman’s room (6), connected to a smaller "basking room" (7) that contains the hero's initial tomb.
*   **The Left Wing (The Tomb Complex):** The western side of the map is a complex network of rooms.
    *   **Bottom Left:** A preparation room (8) contains a cage, which leads into a pillar-filled room (9) and a web-filled spider room (10). 
    *   **Middle Left:** A storeroom (11) acts as a bottleneck, containing a crank mechanism. This connects to a small swinging bridge (12) that crosses over a chasm to reach the inner sanctum.
    *   **Top Left (The Inner Sanctum):** This area contains the locked tomb (13) and a previously looted tomb (14).
    *   **The Heart (Far Top Left):** The end of the dungeon is the sacrifice room (15), a large square room with a raised perimeter walkway and a pit in the center. Behind this is the final hero's tomb chamber (16).

**Spatial Navigation Note:** If a player enters from the dock at the bottom right, they must traverse the water/bridge section to get to the "tomb" side on the left. The left side is essentially a series of chambers requiring players to navigate through guard rooms (8, 9, 10, 11) to unlock access to the final tomb areas (13, 14, 15, 16).
