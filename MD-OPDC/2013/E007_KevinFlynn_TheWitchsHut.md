### 2. Text Transcription

**The Witch’s Hut**
**by Kevin Flynn**

**Adventure Hook**
A rumour from the locals suggests that the nearby Witch has been seducing all the men using an amulet that makes her irresistible, even though she is old and ugly. No one is able to stop her as every time they try to act against her she either vanishes inside her hut or seduces all the men. The women of the region will pay to have her removed.

**The Environment**
The witch's hut is out on the peat bog; the ground is soft and clings to you, leaving a dark sticky layer over your feet and legs. People in heavy armour will quickly become bogged and unable to move. To advance they must shed the weight (up to GM how far they want to take this).

The peat bog has a very gentle wave to it, with small rises and dips, which the witch uses to conceal her hut. A bunch of low growing, spindly trees grow around the hut. The hut looks like an upside-down bird’s nest, a mass of branches and mud standing some 9 feet high, curving down to a diameter of 12 feet. A single entrance, covered by a tattered, multi-layered, dark brown curtain, is the only way in. A gentle yellow light leaks out through gaps. Entering via any other means results in 100% reflection (you hit a magical barrier).

**Inside the Hut**
The inside is much bigger: 20ft high and 30ft wide. It is dominated by 4 features:
1.  **A Table (Center):** Contains a half-eaten meal, a copper mug of mead, and a chair with three engravings: a snowflake, a flame, and a "Z" (sleep rune).
2.  **A Cupboard (Left):** 8ft high by 6ft wide. Opening the drawer yields winter coats. Opening the doors leads to a "Winter Wonderland" pocket dimension.
3.  **A Fireplace (Opposite):** 6ft by 6ft. Standing in the fire transports the player to a "Cavern of Fire" pocket dimension.
4.  **A King-Sized Bed (Right):** Smells of sensuality. Players must strip to bedclothes, climb in, and close their eyes to enter a "Dream Land" pocket dimension.

**The Challenges**
*   **The Winter Wonderland:** A freezing environment. Players must battle Yetis to retrieve the metal disc (snowflake emblem) located on a tree stump 100ft away.
*   **The Cavern of Fire:** A lava-filled cavern. Players must traverse the rock to a pedestal on the far side to retrieve the metal disc (flame emblem) while battling fire elementals.
*   **The Dream Land:** A bizarre landscape of giant eyeballs on stalks. Players must avoid acid tears from flying eyeballs and defeat an "Eye Lord" (Beholder) to retrieve the metal disc (Z-rune).

**The Climax**
Once all three discs are placed on the chair engravings, the witch appears. She is a clerical caster (treat as a Hag). Her first attack is a mass charm (Wisdom save); if failed, the victim returns to bed. Upon her death, she casts a "dying curse" causing all the party's financial transactions to cost 20% more for one year. Her body holds a *Talisman of Attraction*, which boosts charisma-based social skills but makes the wearer uncontrollably seductive to the village women.
