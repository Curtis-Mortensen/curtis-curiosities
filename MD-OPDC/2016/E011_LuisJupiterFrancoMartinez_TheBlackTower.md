### Map Description
The dungeon is organized into a modular hub-and-spoke system.

1.  **The Ground Floor:** A central square room acting as the main hub. It has four corridors leading out of it (North, South, East, West). When players walk into these corridors, they are magically transported to one of the other rooms based on the "Shadows Walk Table."
2.  **The Rooms (Satellite Encounters):** There are several room layouts, each themed by color (Red, Blue, Green, Yellow).
    *   **The Maze:** A 7x7 grid featuring a winding path that snakes from one corner to another. 'L' (Lacryma) tokens are placed at the dead ends.
    *   **The Cells:** A 6x4 room grid. Two rows of small cells line the sides, with a "Statue" in the center of the walkway.
    *   **Pool and Garden:** 5x5 open grid rooms. The Pool rooms (Red and Blue) feature a large central 'L' (Lacryma) in the water. The Garden rooms (Green and Yellow) feature patches of "Roses" and a central 'L'.
    *   **The Library:** A 7x7 room with four tables placed symmetrically in the corners. In the center is an 'R' (Riddle) marker next to a statue. These rooms are guarded by Gargoyles ('G').
    *   **Generic Rooms:** Simple 7x7 empty grid spaces, also populated with Gargoyles ('G').
3.  **The Goal (The Core):**
    *   **Antechamber:** A wide, symmetrical hall with pillars and a central pathway. This is guarded by four "wraith sorcerers."
    *   **Master's Chamber:** A large, complex laboratory space at the top of the tower.
    *   **Treasure Room:** A smaller, separate, adjacent space that only becomes accessible once the master is defeated. 

**Navigation Note:** Players do not "walk" between these rooms traditionally. They enter a corridor from the Ground Floor, roll on the table, and are "teleported" to the room. Once they have collected the required items (two Lacrymas of every color), they gain access to the Antechamber.
