**The Wizard’s Star**
*One Page Dungeon by Stephan Lucu & Ryan Mackenzie*

**Introduction**
You and your fellow companions awaken to find yourself face to face with an apparition of an old wizard. The apparition seems to be a sort of magical recording repeating itself over and over again. It says, “Venture through each door of color and face what lies within. Only after each color has been conquered shall freedom of my pocket plane be given.”

**Room contains:** One gold coin, a dagger, a full waterskin, a small branch, and a bone.

**Completion Reward:** When all rooms are completed, the apparition will disappear and a portal will form to release the party from the pocket plane. The adventurers receive a magical crystal that enables them to teleport to the Wizard’s pocket plane once a day upon completion of the five rooms.
