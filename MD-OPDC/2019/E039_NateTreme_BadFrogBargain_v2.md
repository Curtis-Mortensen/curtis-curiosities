**Bad Frog Bargain**
*By Nate Treme*

**Lore:**
Sigwin, a minor Elfland lord, promised to make the frogling Tobbit mayor of Burdelen in exchange for his first-born child. Since becoming mayor and having a son, Tobbit has banned any elves or other fae creatures from entering Burdelen. Sigwin is determined to collect his payment and raise a frog child as his own in Elfland. He has cursed the town’s water source, the Ancient Well, causing a 1 in 6 chance of mutating anyone who drinks from it. He has also summoned a magic dark cloud over the town that rains dangerous things once per day. He has sent a letter to Tobbit stating that the curses will stop if he drops the child into the well after walking around it widdershins, or counter-clockwise. (This opens a portal to Elfland).

Since becoming mayor 2 years ago, Tobbit has spread rumors about fae creatures trying to steal babies and has fomented a strong anti-fae sentiment in the town. He eventually banned all faeriekind from Burdelen. His wife, Morva, has been mutated and is on forced bedrest. He hasn’t told anyone about his deal with Sigwin. If confronted by the PCs about it, he will offer them gold and a town festival in their honor if they solve his problem. 

Sigwin camps on a hill overlooking the town. Four trees around his tent are really Bark Golems. He offers a bag of ancient faerie coins and a silver Faerie Bell to anyone who delivers the baby frog to him. He will offer to put a glamour on any fae creatures so they appear as humans unless closely inspected.

### Map Description for Gameplay

There are two primary maps to consider for this scenario:

#### 1. The Town of Burdelen
The map is a perfect circle representing the town walls.
*   **Perimeter:** A circular fortified wall surrounds the entire town.
*   **Internal Layout:** It is structured like a wagon wheel. Streets radiate from the center toward the outer wall.
*   **Center:** The "Mayor’s House" is located at the absolute center of town.
*   **The Well:** Just outside the Mayor’s house, sitting in the central hub of the town, is the "Ancient Well."
*   **The Hill:** Sigwin’s tent is located outside the circular walls on a nearby hill, which is illustrated just to the left of the town circle.

#### 2. The Mayor’s House (Floor Plan)
This is a small, five-room structure. Visualize it as a rectangle split into smaller segments:
*   **Room 1 (Parlor):** Located at the front/bottom of the building; this is the main entrance.
*   **Room 2 (Office):** Located directly above the Parlor.
*   **Room 3 (Nursery):** Located to the right of the Parlor/Office section.
*   **Room 4 (Bedroom):** Located below the Nursery (bottom right corner).
*   **Room 5 (Hidden Room):** This is a long, thin room that runs horizontally along the "back" of the building, connecting the space behind the Office (Room 2) and the Nursery (Room 3).

**Secret Passages:** The map notes that access to the Hidden Room (Room 5) is located in the Office (Room 2) behind a bookshelf, and in the Nursery (Room 3) behind a tapestry.
