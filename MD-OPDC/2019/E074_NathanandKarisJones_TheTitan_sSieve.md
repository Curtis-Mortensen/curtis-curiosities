### Map Description & Gameplay Guide
This dungeon functions as a "sorting facility" that separates adventurers from their gear.

**The Setup (The Centrifuge):**
Players start in the center of a large, circular chamber with three concentric rings (fences).
*   **Ring 1 (Inner):** Gold and Potions.
*   **Ring 2 (Middle):** Weapons, Items, Corpses, and Armor.
*   **Ring 3 (Outer):** Rats (Large, Medium, and Magical).
*   **Mechanic:** Players begin in the center. Combat lasts 3 rounds against rats that enter from the outer ring. At the end of round 3, the floor opens, and players drop into a specific chamber based on their properties.

**The Separation (The Chambers):**
Once the floor drops, players are sorted into one of three areas based on their state:
1.  **The Sifter:** Small/Tiny characters or loose items land here. They can find gold and potions. Roll 1d8 to determine the potion's properties (e.g., Levitation, Petrification).
2.  **The Cleaning Room:** Bodies (and adventurers) fall into a pool of water with flesh-eating worms and rusty chains. After 3 rounds, the room "flushes." Players can find gear pieces (Helm, Coat, Belt, Boots) that carry specific magical adjectives (Violence, Terror, Art, Love).
3.  **The Maginet:** Mages and magic items are pulled here by a magnet. Mages must roll 1d4; a 1-2 means they are stuck to the wall. Items found here are rolled on two tables: one for general items and one for magical effects (Binding, Voices, Truth, Plague).

**The Final Escape:**
Once players have survived their sorting chamber, they must choose an exit to leave the Titan’s body:
*   **The Toes:** A combat-heavy exit. Players must fight through 1d8+3 giant, toxic, wriggling toes.
*   **The Torso:** An athletic/speed challenge. It takes 4 rounds to cross the body, but the Titan rolls over in 1d4 rounds (forcing a "dodge" check).
*   **The Ear:** A stealth-based exit. Players must creep past the ear while looking down at the Titan's brain (suggesting a potential optional boss fight or objective).
