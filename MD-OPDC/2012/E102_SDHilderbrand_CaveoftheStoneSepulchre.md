### Map Description for Playability

This dungeon is roughly organized from a bottom-right entrance into several interconnected wings.

**The Entrance and Lagoon (Bottom Right):**
You begin at the bottom-right, where the entrance is marked by foliage leading into "cold pools." A path moves upward past an altar and urns of offerings to a "black pudding" hazard. This leads to a subterranean lagoon (labeled with depths of 20', 25', and 30') inhabited by a "tentacle beast."

**The Upper Right Wing:**
From the lagoon, you can head north through stone steps into a large cavern containing "woven baskets of offerings," "clay pots," and "calcified skeletons." This leads to a "crystallized ladder" and a crevice area. A northern tunnel leads to a pit area filled with bats, where an "ornate dagger on a ledge" is visible.

**The Northern and Central Passages:**
The top of the map features a winding, narrow corridor with "spikes" and a "gibbering mouther." To the far west of this is a high-elevation area with a waterfall, a grate, and a "chute" that leads down to an "uphill climb."

**The Kruthik Hive (Center):**
In the center of the map is a cluster of natural, organic-shaped chambers. This is the Kruthik nest, featuring the "Kruthik hive lord," "Kruthik eggs," and "young Kruthiks." A "crawl space" leads out of this area toward "loot."

**The King’s Sepulchre (Mid-Left):**
West of the hive is a rectangular chamber containing the "King and family" and "offering basins." This room is bordered to the south by a "quiz room" with a "magic mouth" and a hall with six alcoves and six altars. Further south is a circular room featuring a "golden condor" statue and "arrow slits overlooking mountains"—the potential "EXIT."
