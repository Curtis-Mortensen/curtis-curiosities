The lair is lit by eternal torches. Thieves will have concealed their picks and tools on their person and spell casters have their components and holy symbols hidden in their clothing. Rations for one day and a wineskin filled with water will be dropped down for each party member.

**Room Descriptions:**
1. **Old Weapons Room:** Rusted weapons. 4 usable weapons can be found if searched. Grindstone available.
2. **Guard Bunk Room:** 6 bunk beds, table, 6 chairs. Long sword and large shield under mattresses.
3. **Captain of Guards Room:** Locked/trapped chest and locker. Contains chain mail, helmets, boots, tinder box, oil, potions of extra healing, and a lantern.
4. **Meeting Room:** Rotten furniture. 6 giant rats attack if provoked.

7. **Zeus Temple:** Statue of a man, eagle statue, shield. Black lightning-bolt tapestries cause electrical damage. Desecration summons an angry goat.
8. **Poseidon Temple:** Statue of a man holding a cup. Statues of horses in corners. Filling the cup with water reveals 5 potions of water breathing. Desecration summons an angry crab.
9. **Family Tomb:** 6 sarcophagi (3 open, 3 sealed). Sealed sarcophagi contain ghasts; one holds the key to the secret door. Secret door on North wall requires two keys (one from ghast, one from Minotaur).
10. **Bar Trap:** Debris-filled. Entering triggers iron bars to trap the room. 2 gelatinous cubes attack. Hidden lever releases bars.
11. **Sleep Room:** Saving throw vs. magic required or sleep for 1-6 rounds. Zombies from room 12 enter this room if PCs fall asleep.
12. **Zombie Room:** 10 zombies attack and pursue.
13. **Spider Room:** Huge spider attacks. Room contains cocoons; one human corpse has leather armor.
14. **Fungus Room:** Spore cloud causes a save vs. magic; failure causes character to attack allies for 1-4 rounds.
15. **Underground Pool:** Turtles and an octopus. Point T is an underwater tunnel (30-minute swim).
16. **Mold Room:** Brown mold. Cold damage after 3 rounds.
17. **Minotaur Lair:** Contains a Minotaur, axes, and a grindstone. Minotaur carries the key to the secret door in room 9.

*   **B/B*:** Connects Level I and Level II.
*   **E/E*:** Connects Level II and Level IV.
*   **F/F*:** Connects Level II and Level IV.
