### 2. Transcription

**The Ichor of Vercingetorix (for 5th-7th level characters)**
*by C.M. Lebrun (http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/)*

The sages say that ages ago a Demon Lord by the name of Vercingetorix waged war across the realm, bringing all of mankind to its knees before his devastating horde. As Vercingetorix celebrated his victory in the Abyss, the kings of earth offered him sacrifices and gifts of homage, but Yeenoghu, his little brother, saw a chance for glory, attacking him during his revelry. The power of Vercingetorix’s essence was too powerful to be destroyed, but Yeenoghu was able to separate his immortal pieces. The pieces were then scattered by Yeenoghu’s cultists. Nearly three ages later a rogue Cleric of Yeenoghu, Mefyu, stumbles upon an urn filled with Vercingetorix’s ichor…

**Situation:** The local city has been swelling with refugees due to attacks in the farm country to the south. The local militia and clergy are now looking for a band of adventurers to determine what is causing the attacks and bring it to a stop.
-Rumors reveal that the marauders are humanoid with a host of undead. Many recall the howls, barks, yips, lifeless hisses, and the screams.

**Random Encounters**
*(Wilderness, 1-2 on d10):*
1- 2 Ogres
2- 2 Owlbears
3- 3 Werewolves
4- A Huge Monstrous Spider and 2 Medium Monstrous Spiders
5- A Wyvern
6- A Troll

*(Dungeon, 1 on d10):*
1- 6 Stirges
2- 3 Ghouls and a Ghast (from room 9)
3- 2 2nd lvl Ranger Gnolls with 2 Hyenorgs (stats as a Worg) (from rooms 11 and 5)
4- A Troll (out of room 3)

**One Square = 10 ft**
The dungeon was once an ancient sewer system that Yeenoghu’s cultists added on to. The ancient sewer walls and floors (1-3, 7, 9-10, 18) have all yellowed with ancient filth. The additions (8, 11-17) are all of granite that remains quite clean. Rooms 7 and 9 have filled to a depth of 4ft with stagnant rain water.

**Room Key:**
1. The entrance is easily noticed due to the heavy traffic from it to the pond 100 yards West of it. 6 Stirges wait here for the day to cool enough to hunt.
2. This old sewer collection area has long dried up; a search will discover a skeleton missing its skull. Casting detect magic on it will cause an illusionary skull to appear. “Take anyone of my bones and it will be as a wand of your choice for one day.” (3rd level spells and below) The bone will then disintegrate. After leaving this area the bars mend themselves back into position.
3. A curtain made of mangy hides blocks view into this room. The room reeks of body odor and rotting flesh. Two Trolls call it home; one is always patrolling while the other tends to their pets in rooms 4 and 5.
4. The smell of mange is pungent in this earthen den of 4 Hyenas and 2 Hyenorgs (stats as a Worg).
5. 2 Hyenorgs are 50% patrolling with the watch (see 11)/ 25% asleep/ 25% awake and waiting for a meal.
6. Earthen walls line what appears to be a freshly dug chamber that smells of mange and rotting meat. The growls and yips of 3 Hyenadons suggest you are not welcome here.
7. Dark fetid water gradually comes to a depth of 4ft in the main chamber. Two skeletons lay on the bottom; looting those yields 3 Cure Mod. Wounds Potions. 50% chance that Ghouls and Ghasts from room 9 will investigate activities in 7.
8. This room has chair and tables scattered around with sleeping rolls along the walls. A 3rd lvl Gnoll Barbarian and 2 3rd level Gnoll Rangers are gambling over a child tied up in the corner. The child is Mary Ellen, daughter of Constable Ellen from three towns over.
9. 3 Ghasts and 4 Ghouls lie hidden in the 4ft deep water waiting for a meal or orders. Anyone other than the Babau in room 14 or Mefyu will be attacked. They prefer to drag their prey down into the water before eating them.
10. A broken ladder hangs from the ceiling, but looks like it goes to the surface; an earthen plug blocks the hole above.
11. Beds line the walls of what was once a barracks for the guild that protected the ichor. 2 2nd level Gnoll Rangers are 50% patrolling on watch (see 4)/ 25% asleep/ 25% awake and eating.
12. This room is the same design as 11; this room is home to a 3rd level Gnoll Cleric tending to a 4th lvl Half-Fiend Gnoll Barbarian, fresh from the metamorphosis.
13. A Kitchen where 5 Human slaves are held under the watchful eyes of 3 3rd lvl Gnoll Rangers.
14. This dark room smells of sulfur and brimstone; it was once used for profane rituals, but now is home to a sly Babau.
15. The urn that contained the ichor sits on an altar. The urn acts as a cursed iron flask sucking in whoever opens it. Remove Curse cancels the curse for one day.
16. This room is baroque in its decorations paying homage to Yeenoghu; the images of slaughter and carnage gleam in the eyes of 3 Dretches as they scrub the floors.
17. Mefyu’s lavish abode. 4th lvl Half-Fiend Gnoll Cleric; the ichor causes anyone slain by him to rise the next round as a Ghoul (HD<3) or a Ghast (HD 3+), which he commands freely. Wields a dire flail +1 evil/+1 flaming.
18. Similar to room 10 except that the ladder is intact while the hole to the surface is plugged with a foot of earth.

### 3. Map Description for Play
The map is a rectangular grid layout. Imagine it as a series of connected chambers.

*   **Main Sewer Channel:** The dungeon follows a long, vertical corridor running top-to-bottom. Room 1 (the entrance) is in the top-left. It leads down into a series of sewer rooms: Room 2, then Room 3, which opens into a larger chamber (Room 7), and finally into a massive submerged room (Room 9).
*   **The Side Branches:**
    *   To the right of the entrance path are Rooms 4, 5, and 6, which branch off the main sewer line.
    *   Room 10 is a small dead-end chamber at the very top right, representing a sealed exit.
*   **The Granite Wing (The Cultist Addition):** To the right of the main sewer channel is a block of "clean" granite rooms.
    *   Room 8 sits at the top right of this wing.
    *   Below it is a row of rooms: 11, 12, 13, and 14, which connect to each other.
    *   At the bottom right of the map are the inner sanctum rooms: 15 (Altar), 16, and 17 (Mefyu's room).
    *   Room 18 is an isolated room at the far right edge of the complex, also containing a sealed surface exit.

**Connectivity Summary:**
The dungeon is linear at the start (1 → 2 → 3), but opens into the main flood zone (7 → 9). The "Granite Wing" (8–17) is a self-contained block that connects to the main sewer line, likely through doors or hallways between the sewer section (3/7/9) and the cultist section (8/11/14). When running this, treat the main sewer as a wet/filthy path and the granite wing as a dry, elevated, and more structured defensive area for the Gnolls.
