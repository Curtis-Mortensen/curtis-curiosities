### Description of the Map for Accessibility

The "map" is a large, hand-drawn side profile of a human head, facing right. The skull is divided into several distinct, irregular, puzzle-like shapes, mimicking historical phrenology diagrams (which mapped personality traits to specific physical regions of the brain).

*   **The Starting Point:** In the center-top of the forehead area, there is a zone labeled **"Locality"** with the instruction **"Begin here."** This serves as the anchor point for the "dungeon crawl" into the mind.

*   **The Goal:** The "dungeon" is essentially a conversation. By moving through these zones and tracking the results in the 15 boxes at the bottom of the page, the GM determines the final "sanity" or "madness" of the patient based on the accumulation of ticks.
