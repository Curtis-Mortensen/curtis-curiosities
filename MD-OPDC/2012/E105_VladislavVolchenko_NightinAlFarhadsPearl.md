### Part 2: Map Description for Accessibility

The dungeon consists of two maps, designed for a party to navigate in sequence.

#### Map 1: The Caravanserai (Ground Floor)
This map is a U-shaped building complex surrounding a central, open-air **Courtyard (A3)**.
*   **The Entrance:** The front of the complex (the bottom of the map) features the **Main Gates (A1)**. To the right of the gate is the **Gate Control (A2)**.
*   **The Courtyard:** The center of the layout is an open square (A3) containing a central well.
*   **The Outer Ring:**
    *   **Left Wing:** Moving counter-clockwise from the bottom left, there are the **Stables (A4)**, **Storage (A5)**, and the **Chaihana/Tea House (A6)**.
    *   **Top Wing:** The **Resting Chambers (A7)** line the top of the complex.
    *   **Right Wing:** Moving down the right side, there is the **Kitchen (A10)**, the **Baths (A11)**, the **Clinic (A12)**, and finally the **Guard Room (A8)**, which sits in the bottom right corner near the entrance.
    *   **Center Back:** The **PCs' specific Resting Chambers (A9)** are located in the top center, just above the courtyard.

#### Map 2: The Slaver Dungeon (Underground)
This map is more cramped, resembling a subterranean block of cells and chambers connected by narrow corridors.
*   **The Nexus:** The **Trading Hall (B5)** is the largest room, located near the top center, serving as a hub.
*   **The Layout:**
    *   **Top:** The **Slave Barracks (B1F/B1M)** occupy the top left, and the **Zindan (B2)** pit prison is in the top right.
    *   **Mid-section:** The **Trading Hall (B5)** is central, with the **Arena (B6)** situated directly below it.
    *   **Left Wing:** Below the barracks are the **Slavers Barracks (B3)**, **Smithy (B7)**, and **Weaponry (B8)**. The **Elite Guard Room (B4)** is tucked between the barracks and the trading hall.
    *   **Right Wing:** To the right of the central area are the **Slavelord’s Chambers (B10)**, **Storage (B11)**, and the **Arcanist’s Chambers (B12)**.
    *   **Bottom:** The **Kitchen (B9)** is centrally located at the bottom.
    *   **Access:** The **Dungeon Entrance/Stairway (B13)** is located in the bottom-left corner, which acts as the vertical connection back up to the Guard Room (A8) on the surface.
