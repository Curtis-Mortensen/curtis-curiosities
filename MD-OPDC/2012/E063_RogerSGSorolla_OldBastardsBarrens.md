#### Missions for Characters (Levels 2-3)
*   **2-3:** Travel from Ullwick to Sturgate.
*   **4:** Make the trip with a slow cart of goods.
*   **5:** Clear all enemies in range of a trade route between the towns.
*   **6-7:** Clear a realm for a lazy lord; clear all active enemies in range of a 3-hex radius from [X].
*   **8+:** Defeat the lich Oechrus.

#### Encounter Rules
Roll d20 in each 6-hour period and each time a new hex is entered. This tells you which hex to check for an encounter happening in the current hex. Encounter happens if the current hex is within the monster’s range and active time.
*   **1:** Encounter comes from this hex. If entering hex, its lair is found. If not monster’s active time, they are present; if active, there are 2x normal numbers.
*   **2-4:** Encounter comes from this hex.
*   **5-6:** Encounter from 1 hex away (d6, start at North & go clockwise).
*   **7-8:** Encounter from 2 hexes away (d12, start at North & go clockwise).
*   **9-10:** Clue to encounter in hex (tracks, sounds, victims).
*   **11:** Clue to 1 hex away (d6).
*   **12-19:** No encounter.
*   **20:** Roll twice.

*(Legend: Numbers in hexes refer to monster range in hexes, with symbols denoting Day, Night, or Both.)*

#### Keyed Locations & Monsters
*   **Ullwick:** The Good Horse Ephyon (as ki-rin, no flying), guardian of Ullwick, helps only in dire need.
*   **Sturgate:** 4 level 4 adventurers led by rogue level 6 Jayne Monson, guard 30 homesteaders in fenced village, play brigands & gnolls against each other.
*   **Tower of Oechrus:** Necromancy lab in basement (blade trap, 4 wights); 1st story (guard room with 6 necrophidii); 2nd story (throne, black pudding); 3rd story (library, demon, clues to disable barrow glyphs).
*   **Abandoned Church:** Altar casts *restoration* 4x/year; under it is a banner of protection vs. undead (10’ radius).
*   **Barrow of Oechrus:** Triumphal arch and stone-paved path to barrow; warnings about waking Oechrus. Barrow sealed by heavy stone. Inside, in a maze of his own lethal glyphs, lies the “old bastard”: dormant lich Oechrus, +4 holy avenger sword through his heart and jeweled medallions (2000$ each) on his eyes. So preserved, he is indestructible. If medallions are removed, undead rise and rally for 3 miles around barrow (500 skeletons, 10 zombies, 10 shadows, 4 wraiths, 2 spectres). If sword is removed, Oechrus awakens!
*   **Brigand Gangs:** 3 gangs, mutual distrust from Monson’s trickery; West: Bastard Sons (30); Middle: Guthangers (35); East: Hellkites (25 mounted women). Patrols of 2d10, cave lairs. Guthangers have oracular skull, Pale Boy Groon.
*   **Other notable locations/entities:** 8 trolls with 200 head of cattle and regenerating bull of infinite steaks; 30 Oechrus cultists with level 7 priest; Banshee Perdita (lover of Oechrus); Wild Waragoon (lone level 4 hunter); 2d6 non-singing harpies; 40 horse tamer nomads; 50' carved face of Oechrus on crag (expression changes daily).
