**Description:** The Putik-kubo usher royalty and their possessions to the afterlife by feeding the deceased to a big gelatinous cube in a bottomless pit in the center of their village, but nobody (including the Putik-kubo) know about the gelatinous cube. The pit is hundreds of feet deep and the village would never peacefully allow access into this holy place. However, a cave at the foot of their mountain home contains a long-forsaken entry to the burial chamber where generations of riches are stolen right from under them.

### Map Description for Play
The dungeon is structured as an isometric, branching path system that converges on a central, large chamber.

*   **Entryway (Area 1):** The adventure starts at the entrance, a cave mouth that splits into two distinct paths: an "Upper Path" and a "Lower Path."
*   **The Upper Path:** This path leads to **Area 2**, a spiked pit room, then connects to **Area 3**, a ruined room, and finally terminates at **Area 4**, which contains a golden door and a trap-triggering key.
*   **The Lower Path:** This path mirrors the upper, leading to **Area 5** (a second spiked pit room), then **Area 6** (a golem room), and terminating at **Area 7**, which houses the second golden door and key.
*   **The Destination (Area 8):** Both the Upper and Lower paths eventually funnel the party into the center of the map: a large, hexagonal room containing the Radiant Giant Gelatinous Cube.
*   **Verticality:** The map uses "footholds" along the pit walls in areas 2 and 5 to imply depth. Area 8 features a vertical shaft reaching upward, visually connecting the dungeon floor to the "surface" level above.
