### Transcription of Text

**Escape from New Goblin City**

You’ve been thrown into the city, which has been abandoned to anarchy, ruin, and the goblin. Stoic wardens watch the city from their towering gates, but order is mainly kept by a balance of power between three factions: Snivel’s unruly mob, Griblet’s bruisers, and Hornswoggle’s mincing accountants. The wardens will not tolerate a united city, knowing it could easily overwhelm the gates.

Control over key locations provides access to resources, expressed in terms of Force, Finance ($), and Faith. Force is supereffective (1.5x) vs. Finance, Finance vs. Faith, and Faith vs. Force (must be ≥ to capture). Each resource can be used once a day, declared at the beginning of the day (allowing time for other factions to react). This is in addition to whatever happens through regular adventure. The three faction leaders will act to defend and expand their positions.

### Map Description for Gameplay
The map is an isometric, black-and-white ink illustration of a high-density, ramshackle city built within a stone wall. 

*   **General Layout:** The city is divided into several districts. The entire area is enclosed by a tall, stone wall. The **Gates** are prominently located at the "top" (northernmost point) of the map. They appear as a fortified structure with tall pillars and a portcullis.
*   **The Northern Section:** Just below the gates, tucked into the top-left corner, is a shadowed, jagged ruin identified as **The Beast’s** lair. It looks like a collapsed structure or cave-mouth.
*   **The Central District:** Dominating the center of the city is **The Market**, depicted as a dense cluster of stalls and tents with winding, narrow alleyways. To the left of the market are the **Fighting Pits**, recognizable by a small circular enclosure.
*   **The Left Flank:** The **Snivel Court** consists of a pair of tall, leaning, multi-story tenements that dominate the mid-left portion of the map.
*   **The Lower/Right Sectors:** 
    *   **Pigurk’s Scrapyard** and **Slubgob’s Fine Meats** are clustered in the lower-left/central area; they appear as industrial-looking buildings with pipes and heavy equipment.
    *   The **City Printing House** is a blocky building in the lower-center. 
    *   The **Monastery** and **Counting House** are located toward the right/bottom right. The Monastery has a pointed roof that mimics a steeple, while the Counting House looks like a sturdy, administrative stone building. 
    *   The **House of Charity** is situated toward the lower right, appearing as a slightly smaller, more humble structure.
*   **Navigability:** The map is highly dense with "labyrinthine" architecture. Players should imagine the streets as narrow, winding passages that connect these major landmarks. The terrain is "goblincore"—messy, cluttered, and vertically chaotic.
