**SETTING/DESCRIPTION:**

### Map Description for Gameplay
The "Infernal Contraption" is a 60' x 60' building that operates as a 3D puzzle rather than a flat map.

**The Grid Layout:**
The building is broken into three distinct floors (Upper, Middle, Lower). Each floor is represented by a 3x3 grid (9 squares total), where each square is a 20'x20' room.
*   **Vertical Alignment:** The rooms are vertically stacked. For example, if you are in room U1, you are directly above M1, which is directly above L1.
*   **The Leg Mechanic:** When the Contraption "activates," the four corners of the grid (1, 3, 7, and 9 on each level) become the "limbs." U1/M1/L1 together form the front-left leg; U3/M3/L3 form the front-right leg, and so on.

**The Transformation Diagrams:**
There are three states of operation for the machine, illustrated in the side views:
1.  **The Tenement (At Rest):** A simple 6-story (60' tall) rectangle with false brick walls hiding the machinery.
2.  **The Terrible Tractor (Legs extended 1 block):** The building rises to 80' tall. The bottom level (L) extends outward, exposing the tracked feet. The Arcano-Engines (L4/L6) engage the knee gearboxes in the middle level (M).
3.  **The Walking Warspite (Legs extended 2 blocks):** The building rises to 100' tall. The full legs are extended, engaging at the hips (U1, U3, U7, U9). The building now "strides" across the city on these massive limbs, using the track-covered feet as soles.

**Tactical Implications:**
*   The center square of each floor (U5, M5, L5) is the core of the building, featuring the elevator and foyer.
*   Combat or encounters in the corner rooms (1, 3, 7, 9) may be affected by the movement of the legs or the grinding of the gears, depending on which "mode" the Contraption is currently in.
