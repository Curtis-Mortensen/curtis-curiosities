### 3. Map Description (Accessibility Guide)
The map is a 10-foot-per-square grid. The chasm (Room 23) runs vertically through the middle of the map.

**The Goblin Side (Left):**
This side is a rectangular complex.
*   **Top:** Room 1 is in the upper-left corner. Room 2 is a large space in the top middle. Room 3 is a large space on the top right, bordering the chasm.
*   **Middle:** Room 4 is a hallway-like room in the center-left. Room 5 is a small room on the right side of this row, containing the ballista that points across the chasm. Room 6 is to the left of Room 5.
*   **Bottom:** Room 7 is in the bottom-left. Room 8 is in the bottom-middle. Room 9 is a small secret chamber off to the bottom-far-left, accessible only after clearing the other rooms.

**The Kobold Side (Right):**
This side has more irregular shapes and smaller, connected rooms.
*   **Top:** Room 19 is in the upper right. Room 20 is a small space nearby. Room 21 is a very large room in the top-middle right, bordering the chasm. Room 22 is a small tower jutting out into the chasm at the very top, containing the matching ballista.
*   **Middle:** Room 10 is at the top right of this section. Room 11 is adjacent. Rooms 12 and 13 are stacked vertically in the middle. Room 14 is to the right of 13, and Room 15 is to the left. Room 16 is a small secret space tucked near 14.
*   **Bottom:** Room 17 is in the bottom right. Room 18 is a large long room in the bottom-middle.

**Connection:** The "zipline" mechanic connects the ballistas in Room 5 (Left) and Room 22 (Right), allowing travel across the 100-foot chasm.
