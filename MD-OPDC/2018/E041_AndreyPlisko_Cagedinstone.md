### Map Layout Description for Play
This dungeon is designed as a non-linear network of rooms that requires careful navigation, as certain areas are gated by mechanical puzzles.

1.  **The Starting Zone (Top Left):** Players enter in the top-left corner. This area contains the "Entry Point." The path immediately leads to a corridor involving a "Steel Cage" with a Minotaur and a series of "Pit Traps" that require a lever to bypass.
2.  **The Central Hub:** The map radiates from the center. You must navigate through a series of "Floodgates" (airlocks) to manage noise levels.
3.  **Halls of Punishment (Top Center):** A series of chambers meant for combat/torture, guarded by a cross with an old man and orcs. Access here is restrictive—if you carry weapons, you are teleported elsewhere.
4.  **The Eastern Wing (Music Halls):** This area is a puzzle-heavy corridor leading to three distinct rooms: "Drums," "Heavy Metal," and "Opera." Players are subjected to magical tunes here, and failing a will test forces them into one of these rooms.
5.  **The Oracle’s Quarters (Top Right):** Situated near the "Mud Lake," this is a roleplay-heavy area where the party interacts with an unusual group (a girl, an owlbear, a rabbit, and a doll) for lore.
6.  **The Mud Lake (Center Right):** A dangerous zone overseen by a scorpion creature on a bridge; it is occupied by singing mermaids.
7.  **The Cave of Wonders (Bottom Center):** This is the "treasure vault" of the dungeon. It contains the goal of the mission (The Artifact). It requires the players to manipulate the light (darkness) to find the main objective.
8.  **The Final Chamber (Bottom):** Located at the very end of the map, this area is guarded by an Angel Statue. This is the "Exit," where the party is judged on their behavior throughout the dungeon before they can use the portal to escape.
