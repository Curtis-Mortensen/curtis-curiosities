# One Page Dungeon Contest — Judge Rankings & Runners-Up (2010–2020)

Compiled from official contest announcements at [dungeoncontest.com](https://www.dungeoncontest.com), Alex Schroeder-era archives, and published compendium front matter. Use this to cross-reference against the transcribed master list in `MD-OPDC/`.

**Format note:** Through 2013, judges nominated favorites and assigned whimsical **category awards** (no strict 1–2–3 ranking). From 2014, **placement tiers** were used. From 2017 onward, judges scored entries and results are **ranked by total points** (2017 also lists informal “just for fun” categories).

Each line is one dungeon plus a single sentence summarizing why judges singled it out.
