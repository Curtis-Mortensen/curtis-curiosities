**[Lore Box]**
Deep in the mountains north of Everlund in the Forgotten Realms lives a lich named "Archibald Dementinus," he has too much time on his hands and therefore he has created some dungeons to entertain him. He arranges a "kidnapping teleport" of unsuspecting heroes and watches them go through his dungeons like in a maze. He has no interest in killing anyone, but if they are not up for the challenge he doesnt help them either.

Some time ago a shadowy organisation heard rumours about his dungeons and sought him out, for unknown reasons they sometimes pay him to kidnap specific people into his dungeons. Archibald has not been bothered to question their reasons and he doesnt really care as long as he gets his entertainment. Pride is his biggest weakness, as such he left his markings all about his dungeons.

Archibald lived in Baldurs gate, the son of a common blacksmith, gifted with a great intellect and a desire to not end up like his father. He sought out wizards to become apprentice and learn the arcane, but was often turned down until he met Kraktzol Vianko. Beneath the masquerade of being a regular wizard, Kraktzol was researching necromantic powers and as time went by Archibald grew in power and was soon sent out on missions to fetch Kraktzol specific ingredients. "It was during one of his missions Archibald discovered what his old master was about, lichdom and greed got the better of Archibald. He had a few key ingredients pretending to fail in getting them for his master and waited until he was powerful enough to cast down and kill his master. The ensuing fight between them tore apart most of the old masters tower and when Archibald stood victorious he quickly gathered all he could and fled north. Eventually he achieved the lichdom his old master had sought and settled in an ancient dwarven keep long abandoned deep inside the mountains near Everlund in the silvermarches."
