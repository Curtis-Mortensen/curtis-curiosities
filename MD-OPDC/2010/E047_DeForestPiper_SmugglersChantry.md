### Text Transcription

#### **Smuggler’s Chantry**
*By DeForest Piper | An Adventure for levels 2 to 4*
Smuggler’s Chantry can be placed within—or near—any town or city. An outlawed chaotic cult has established a fake church (called the Chantry) as a cover for a band of smugglers. The cult uses the neutral church to conceal the activity of the smugglers. The cult uses the smugglers to bring acolytes and sacrifices in without the authorities finding out. Most of the smugglers are unaware that the cult is real.

#### **Rumors**
*If desired, the characters can attempt to find out information about the cult. Roll d6 add Charisma modifier to find highest rumor available to PC. PCs find d4 rumors within their reach for each 6 hours spent searching the town.*

1. Poor people have been vanishing from the area around the Chantry (true)
2. The Chantry is mind washing people (false)
3. Black hell beasts seen in the city (true)
4. Smuggling has been on the rise (true)
5. The Chantry is a benevolent group (sortta)
6. Merchant Dibble is offering a 500 gp reward for the information of his daughter Sheddra who was recently kidnapped (true)
7. There is a new cult in town (true)
8. The Mayor is part of this new cult (false)
9. The Chantry is a cover for slavers and a person can sell slaves to Poxtil (true – Poxtil will buy slaves as sacrifices)

#### **Wandering Monsters**
*Roll a d6 +1 for each loud noise within the last 4 turns. Check every 4 turns.*

**During the day (the basement and dungeon):**
1. d6 Chantry followers (Normal Humans)
2. Kelpis
3. d4 Smugglers
4. d6 Chantry followers (Normal Humans, Drunk)
5. Kelpis
6. Rothius
7. d4 Human Cultists
8. Poxtil (from area 11 investigating noise)

**During the Night and in the lower levels:**
1. 1d4 giant rats
2. 1d4 Human Cultists
3. 1d6 giant rats
4. Rothius
5. Poxtil
6. 1d8 Smugglers
7. Edric
8. Orind
*Named NPCs will call for help and flee if attacked.*

#### **Room Descriptions**
**Surface Level:** Uniformly dark and close, filled with shadows. During the day no one will stop the characters from exploring. People will take note but will not sound an alarm. (1-4 on a d6) a fervent local will extol the wonders of the Chantry.
1. **Altar Room:** Chantry rituals. Rothius is usually here drinking.
2. **Stairway:** Empty. Dwarf PC may notice filled-in arrow slits.
3. **Stairway:** Used to store robes/equipment. Door locked.
4. **Rothius’ Room:** Bedchamber with fireplace. 70 gp hidden in chest.
5. **Hall:** Open area, very dark.
6. **Kitchen:** Well-stocked. Kelpis sleeps here at night.
7. **Porch:** Open during day, locked at night. Filled with smugglers (2d6) and locals (2d6).

**Basement Level:** No light. 15ft ceilings. Spider webs. Intruders will be attacked.
8. **Ossuary:** Niches filled with human bones. Contains a sack with 5 opals (200gp each) and 4d100gp in random items. Old filled-in stairwell.
9. **Stairway:** Empty. Not original to building.
10. **Stairway:** Empty.
11. **Poxtil’s Room:** Spartan bedchamber.
12. **Spider Room:** 8 large spiders (1-1HD, poison). Surprise on 1-4.
13. **Armory:** Weapons stored here.
14. **Barracks:** 2d4+4 Smugglers and 2d4+2 cultists.
15. **Edric the Red:** Sumptuous sleeping chamber. 5 pieces of jewelry (125gp each), jeweled chess set (500gp). 

**Dungeon Level:** Strangely warm, very dark, no cobwebs. 20ft ceilings.
16. **Orind’s Room:** Opulent. 7 statues of obscene gods (400gp).
17. **Ceremony Room:** Sacrifices fed to 2 Hell Hounds. Black stone and bloodstains.
18. **Storage Room:** Robes and captives. Contains Sheddra.

#### **New Monsters**
*   **Edric the Red:** Smuggler leader – Fighter 4, Chaotic(evil), +2 short sword, +1 buckler.
*   **Human, Cultist:** +2 morale, fight to the death. HD: 1+2, AC: varies.
*   **Human, Smuggler:** Treat as Pirates – If cult discovered, ½ lose heart (–2 morale).
*   **Orind:** MU-5; Chaotic(evil); Spells: Charm Person, Magic Missile, Invisibility, Mirror Image, Hold Person; Ring of Protection +2; Ring of Levitation.
*   **Kelpis:** Smuggler Lieutenant. Treat as Human, Sergeant of 3HD.
*   **Poxtil:** Cult Lieutenant. MU-3; Chaotic(evil); Spells: Charm Person, Detect magic, Detect Invisible.
*   **Rothius:** MU-3; Neutral; Spells: Ventriloquism, Hold Portal, ESP; Ring of Charm Person.
