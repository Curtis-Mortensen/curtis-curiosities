### Map Description for Players

The map is designed on a square grid, with rooms connected by straight corridors. Imagine the facility as three connected zones.

*   **The Upper Tower (Northwest):** The entrance is at Room 1 (the tower), which leads down via a trapdoor (Room 2). Room 2 is a large, circular room. From here, a hallway leads east.
*   **The Laboratory Core (Center):** This is a network of squares and corridors. There are several central junctions (marked "4").
    *   To the west of the central junctions are the Living Quarters (Rooms 6, 7, and 8).
    *   To the east of the central junctions are the Pantry and Kitchen (Rooms 9 and 10).
    *   The central-most area contains the Dining Hall (Room 11) and the Lab (Room 12).
    *   To the east of the lab, you will find the Workshop (Room 15) and Reagents Store (Room 14), separated by the spike trap hallway (Room 13).
*   **The Lower/Tavern Section (South):** This area is connected to the central lab by a long, narrow "Roughly hewn" tunnel (Room 17) that runs south from the lab area.
    *   This tunnel connects to a small closet (Room 18) and eventually leads to a basement space containing a storage area (Room 19).
    *   Beyond the storage area is the Taproom (Room 20), representing the tavern cellar.
