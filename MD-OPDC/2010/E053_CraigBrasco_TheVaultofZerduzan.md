**Intro:** Designed and constructed by the demonologist Zerduzan as a burial shrine for all his most favored servants and companions. It is located under the ruins of a tower. Others say this place was a sanctum for Zerduzan's efforts to command demons.

**Room Legend:**
1. **Entrance:** Accessed via a steel ladder. A magic mouth activates the main entrance if its riddle is answered; three failures trigger a disintegration ray.
2. **Homage Gallery:** Four statues of companions. The second (Tyriana) can be pushed to open a secret door to Room 3.
3. **Keepsake Chamber:** A replica of Tyriana's study. Contains a trapped chest with a poison needle (ages victim 3d20 years).
4. **Henchman Chamber I:** Helmed skulls on shelves.
5. **Partially Flooded Room:** Rotted furniture; back section is collapsed, depressed, and flooded.
6. **Henchman Chamber II:** Similar to #4. Opening the north door manifests a wight (elite guard).
7. **Griggleef's Alcove:** Stuffed hippogriff on a darkwood base; leather saddle and harness stored in a chest on the south wall.
8. **Xurisan's Alcove:** A "stuffed" huge winged serpent that is actually in suspended animation. Awakens if non-Zerduzan enters for >2 minutes. Secret door in lower east corner.
9. **Trophy Room:** Shelves with 40 jars containing enemies' heads. "Frithfell" jar has a ring under the tongue; "Oscail" jar opens a secret section of shelves. Secret door in south wall.
10. **Partially Flooded Room:** Rotted demon tapestries; partially flooded.
11. **Art Gallery:** Paintings/busts of Zerduzan. Northeast corner contains a massive cobweb and a huge abyssal spider.
12. **Creepy Antechamber:** Light malfunctions; inhabited by a crazed imp.
13. **Damondech's Crypt:** Revenant lieutenant and six guards. Secret door in southwest corner.
14. **Damondech's Treasure:** Chests with coins, gems, and magic items.
15. **The Crypt of the Rammels:** Twin clerics in sarcophagi. Disturbing them spawns a guardian devil from the north wall.
16. **Empty Antechamber:** Feels full of dread.
17. **Tyriana's Crypt:** Powerful specter (Zerduzan's lover). Secret door in northeast wall.
18. **Cave of Corpses:** Two dozen felt-wrapped corpses. Standing here for >3 minutes animates them as zombies.
19. **Empty Cavern:** Bones, debris, and rat-like creatures. Walls have mangled, jeering corpses.
20. **Slaad's Den:** Silk bed, texts on magic/demonology, and an incense brazier.
21. **Slaad's Cavern:** A powerful Grey Slaad is magically imprisoned.
22. **Zerduzan’s Cave:** Secret laboratory; contains an imprisoned greater demon. Defeating it leaves a petrified heart. Also contains a hidden case with a spell book, robe, bracers, and wand.

### Map Description for Gameplay
The map is a sprawling, interconnected stone dungeon layout. To navigate, think of it as a central cluster of corridors and rooms.

*   **The Main Entry:** You begin at the bottom center (Room 1), which leads immediately north into the **Homage Gallery (2)**. This gallery acts as a central hub for the left side of the complex.
*   **The Western Wing:** From Room 2, you can head west into the **Keepsake Chamber (3)**, or north into the **Henchman Chambers (4 and 6)**. Room 5, a large, partially flooded chamber, sits between them.
*   **The Northern Loop:** Beyond Room 6, the map expands into a series of smaller alcoves and crypts. You progress through the **Hippogriff Alcove (7)**, the **Serpent Alcove (8)**, and the **Trophy Room (9)**. From the Trophy Room, you can head east into a sequence: **Room 10 (Flooded)**, the **Art Gallery (11)**, and the **Creepy Antechamber (12)**.
*   **The Crypts (North-East):** This is the heart of the "vault" proper. It includes the **Damondech Crypt (13)**—with its treasure vault (14) tucked behind it—and the **Rammels Crypt (15)**.
*   **The Inner Sanctum:** Further north and east is the **Empty Antechamber (16)** leading into **Tyriana's Crypt (17)**. 
*   **The Cave Systems (Right Side):** The right side of the map represents a rougher, more organic cave system rather than finished stone. Access to this starts from the eastern portion of the dungeon. It consists of a large, winding path that moves from the **Cave of Corpses (18)** down into the **Empty Cavern (19)**. This leads into the final, high-threat zone consisting of the **Slaad’s Den (20)**, the **Slaad’s Cavern (21)**, and finally **Zerduzan’s Laboratory (22)**, which is the northernmost point of the dungeon.

**Navigation Tip:** Watch for the "S" symbols on the walls—these indicate secret doors, which are essential for creating shortcuts between rooms like 2 and 3, or accessing the hidden treasures in rooms 9, 13, and 17. The "e" symbols mark where enchanted torches are mounted on the walls.
