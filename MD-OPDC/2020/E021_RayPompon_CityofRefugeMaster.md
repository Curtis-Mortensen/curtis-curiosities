**Title:** Flight to the City of Refuge
**Subtitle:** A pointcrawl for 1-3 Low Level Characters
**Author:** By Ray Pompon – http://www.planetheidi.com

**Introduction:**
You’re wandering through a low-magic, stone-age coastal region when you come to a kingdom ruled by a powerful King. His strict code of laws is enforced by a brutal cadre of helmeted warriors. Unfortunately, one of you has violated the sacred law by (roll) and sentenced on the spot to (roll). 

Your only chance, a villager tells you, is the City of Refuge, several days travel away. Safe within its walls, all sins are forgiven, all crimes pardoned. Follow the path to the north to the coast or cut through the untamed jungles or over perilous mountains to evade your pursuers. Whatever you choose, you must leave now, the enforcers are coming!

**Roll your crime:**
1. Yawning while the king made a speech
2. Letting your heathen shadow fall on a shrine
3. Eating the wrong fruit in the wrong season
4. Picking a flower from the sacred garden
5. Smiling at one the king’s wives
6. Fishing in the royal pond

**Roll your sentence:**
1. Stoning
2. Clubbing
3. Drowning
4. Strangulation
5. Burning
6. Thrown off cliff

**Rules:**
*   **Escape:** Roll travel events for each space as you move through it; keep track of spaces per day. Any in metal armor slows whole party by half.
*   **Provisions:** Players have 1-3 days of provisions. After 2nd day w/o, take -1 penalty on all rolls, cumulative per day.
*   **Optional complications:**
    *   **If Lost:** Roll 1D6 = 1-4 stay there, 5 go back previous, 6 go random direction.
    *   **Ticking clock:** Each day after first, 1 in 6 chance of group of enforcers catching up, increased by 1 per day.

**Finds:**
*   **Forage:** Gain 1-3 days provisions.
*   **Trader:** Buy provisions: 1D3 random weapons, 1D6 random items.
*   **Shrine:** Leave valuable, roll 1D3: 1 - relief (provision reset), 2 - heal the most hurt, 3 - bless all for a day.

**Foes:**
*   **Rock Worm:** Giant worm pops out of burrow, roll surprise.
*   **Marsh Lizard:** As giant lizard.
*   **Jungle Beastmen:** 2-3 attack, stat as Lizardmen.
*   **Giant Spider Crabs:** 1-3 erupt from sand, stat as giant scorpion (minus sting).
*   **Enforcers:** 2-4 catch up. They are merciless, relentless, and unspeaking save their constant war chants. Stat as high-str mid-level fighters with spiked clubs (mace), leather armor. Chants and enchanted wood helms gives protection vs. mind-attacks such as charm and sleep.

**Travel events (roll each column for each space entered):**

The map is a network of nodes connected by lines (paths). To "play" this map, you navigate from node to node.

1.  **The Start:** The journey begins at a "Start" icon in the bottom-left corner.
2.  **The Goal:** The "City of Refuge" is represented by a large, fortified tower structure at the top-center of the map.
3.  **Geography:**
    *   **Bottom area:** Primarily "Road" and "Jungle" terrain.
    *   **Mid-left:** A cluster of "Marsh" nodes.
    *   **Mid-right:** "Grass" nodes and a small "Road" junction.
    *   **Top/Central:** The route leads upward through "Coast" terrain toward the city.
    *   **Right edge:** A vertical section of "Rocky" terrain leading toward the top.
4.  **Navigation:**
    *   From the **Start**, you travel north along a road.

    *   The **Coast** occupies the entire top arc of the map, directly bordering the City of Refuge.
    *   The **Rocky** terrain is a "trap" area on the right side, which eventually loops back into the main road system.
5.  **Visual Landmarks:**
    *   There is a **Spider Crab** sketch near the coast area.
    *   A **Beastman** sketch is in the jungle.
    *   A **Rock Worm** sketch is in the rocky area.
    *   A hooded, skeletal figure (the Enforcer) stands at the bottom right, marking the encroaching threat.
