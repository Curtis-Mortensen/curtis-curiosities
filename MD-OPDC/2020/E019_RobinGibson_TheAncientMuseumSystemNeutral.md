**The Ancient Museum**
"Once a place of wonder, known across the world. Thought to be destroyed long ago. Recently a dangerous group of bandits has begun using it as a hideout..."

#### Room Descriptions
*   **Quarters:** 3 Bandits are resting here. Beds and chairs are arranged haphazardly. A mildewy boudoir painting in a gold frame hangs on the Southeast wall (10gp). All unarmored, with daggers. Dog: Foamy, flea-ridden basset hound. Likes apples.
*   **Guardroom:** Bandits sit around table playing cards by candlelight. Won't notice quiet intruders. Bookshelves line the walls. Most books are solid blocks of mycelium under the cover, but 13 are readable (1 in 6 chances of good book). Bandits will set fire to the books if they see intruders taking them. 2 locked chests.
*   **Kitchen:** Boss and Chef are having a shouting match over the quality of ingredients. Two long tables set with nice china (10gp total). Chef is chopping onions on a large stone counter; a wild boar is roasting in the corner. Sacks of dry goods and barrels of ale are scattered along the walls.
*   **Scullery:** A bandit sits on a bench reading a scandalous novel and dozing off. Prisoners are chained by manacles to a heavy metal loop on the North wall. The South end is dominated by a pile of filthy laundry and a sudsy cauldron fed from a hot spring above.
*   **Fossil Room:** Large dark room. 30 fossils are set in the walls. Each will take 1 turn to remove without breaking. 6 piles of coprolite (10gp) on floor. Both dinosaur skeletons awaken if touched, and crumble if defeated. If 3 columns are broken, ceiling will collapse in D4 rounds.
*   **Mummy Room:** A mummy sits in a chair by a fireplace. Trapped by wards. Bodies of previous visitors are twisted into grizzly sculptures. Will allow party to pass if one stays as a companion. 10 books.
*   **Stash:** Behind fire illusion in hearth. Gold snake with tiny ruby scales (100gp; owner will die in D20 days unless sold) and 3 health potions.
*   **Vault:** Door latch hidden behind painting of Boar. 3 sacks of spices (pepper, nutmeg, paprika; 10gp, 20gp, and 30gp) and 3 locked chests.
*   **Boss' Quarters:** Door has complicated lock (takes 3x longer to pick). Bed covered in blue silk sheets/pillows (10gp total). Full-length silver mirror (50gp). Desk with partially finished ship in a bottle. Steel lockbox containing 30 lodestone crossbow bolts (+1 vs metal armor), brass sextant (50gp), compass (20gp), 6 nautical books (50gp each), and 50gp.
*   **Planetarium:** Ghosts dance across floor. They are harmless, but bandits won't go downstairs in fear (may still attack with bows). Functional orrery with 7 brass spheres rotates 10' overhead. Large door to south opens if planets are aligned. Word "syzygy" is engraved on door. If said aloud, it will stop the motion of the spheres.
*   **Catwalk:** Wide stone path looking down on planetarium. 4' railing. Roll D6 for position of patrol.
