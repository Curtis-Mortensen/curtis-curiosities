**Brinewald 1631**
A sandbox for first level PCs set during the 30 Years War
Christian Kessler | popularenchanting.blogspot.com
http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0

**Forest Encounters**
1. thieving gremlin
2. 1d4+1 wolves
3. bear
4. 3d4 murdercrows
5. 1d4+1 bandits
6. shroom addict
7. hermit’s great owl familiar
8. lake creature
*   4 amphibious bipeds live in the lake. They are smart & hungry, as tough as a 5th level fighter, and they have scales as hard as chain mail.
*   The hermit knows a ritual to summon them. He will perform it if he learns the command ritual — or at least thinks he learns it.
*   He spends most of his time producing random spell scrolls, and will trade them for giant ant eggs or other mysterious objects.
*   Anytime the PCs leave the gremlin clearing in any direction, roll 1d4 to see which way they are actually headed.
*   The elder tree is sentient and knows most of what's going on here, but is extremely distrustful of humans until they somehow prove themselves to be allies of the forest. It sees werewolves as champions of nature against civilization.

**Tomb of the Nibelung**
*   A barbarian hero's ghost, who wants her son's remains retrieved from the bottom of the nearby lake.
*   The PCs can tell they can't take her in a fight.
*   She can grant the ability to breathe underwater, but it only works from 12–1 AM.
*   Reward: her burial hoard (enough to advance a lvl 2 party). Also a set of three well-preserved silver weapons (dagger, spearhead, axehead).
*   She's sent adventurers to the lake before, and has no idea why none of them have returned.
*   She has been cooped up in here a long time & only knows about the elder tree and her son.

**Town: Brinewald**
Mayor Heinrich Hoffman; Gunsmith: Gregor Meiers; Blacksmith: Geoff Brineberger; Storekeep: Jakob Guttenberg; Church of St. Hubert (Catholic); Father Hans Dopkenn; Weisehase (White Rabbit) Gasthaus; Inkeep: Markus Borfeld; Barmaid: Anneke Croger; Town Drunk: Arndt Berman.

**Rumors**
1. No one’s seen Franz Heuber lately
2. There’s a reward for dealing with the bandits
3. Demon fish with legs in the lake
4. Pagan ghosts in the forest
5. I ate shrooms & saw the face of Jesus
6. FUCKING GIANT ANTS MAN HOLY SHIT
7. The local wolves have been attacking humans
8. There’s a crazy old heretic living in the woods
9. An old tree near here speaks to witches
10. The Swedes are fucking unstoppable
11. Sgt. Konrad caught a heretic last week
12. Safe haven for witches in a town west of here
*DM: Pick someone to be a werewolf. No one else in town knows about it. The next full moon is in 1d4+1 days (unless you already keep track).*

**Watchtower**
*   Occupants: Sgt Konrad and 2d4+4 militiamen with pikes, muskets, crossbows, and a canon.
*   Konrad only knows the bandits have been seen in the forest; he hasn’t had enough men to go hunting them.
*   He's authorized (by the mayor) to offer a reward, enough to advance a lvl 1 party, to anyone that brings back the bandit leader’s head.
*   He's also authorized to offer smaller rewards (about half the price of a horse) in exchange for fresh wolf pelts.
*   There's a heretic being kept in the dungeon. He's been there for a week and hasn't confessed; the mayor and priest are impatient with Konrad's interrogation methods.
*   The heretic knows about the hermit & the lake.

**Heuber Farmhouse**
*   Living room: front door (smashed), trap door (chained up), Franz Heuber-sized bloodstain, valuable grandfather clock.
*   Bedroom: journal of Franz the insect cultist, describes hive's rest cycle.
*   Kitchen: Ilsabei Heuber (mad with grief/fear) with baby, barricaded door.
*   Basement: ¼ chance of bug encounter/round (1d4-2 ants). Tunnel leads to giant ant colony. 3 kegs of town's favorite beer.

**Giant Ant Colony**
*   2 rest periods/day (8:00AM and 8:00PM), 1d6x30 min long (ants are dormant but twitchy). Queen doesn't rest.
*   1d12+2 ants in each unmarked chamber.
*   Queen controls the hive mind.

### Map Descriptions for Play

**Regional Map (Brinewald)**
The area is defined by the **Mainz River** flowing vertically through the center.
*   **North:** The **Watchtower** and **Bandit Caves** are in the northern sector.
*   **North-East:** The **Tomb of the Nibelung** (a small stone circle) and **1d4 sirens** location.
*   **Center:** A cluster containing the **Gremlin Clearing**, **Elder Tree**, and **Hermit’s House & Garden**.
*   **West:** A **Lumber Camp**.
*   **South-West:** The **Heuber Farm**.
*   **South-East:** A patch of **Shrooms**.

**Bandit Caves**
A cave system with an **Upper Entrance** and a **Lower Entrance**.
*   The path connects to various pockets: a 15' cliff and a 10' cliff define the terrain.
*   There are four distinct bandit groups (sizes 1d8+3, 1d4, 1d2, and the "Bandit Leader with Loot").
*   Hazards: A bear (with a tunnel "too narrow for the bear"), patches of yellow mold, and green slime.

**Tomb of the Nibelung**
A circular room featuring a ghost encounter at the center. It is surrounded by a lake area.

**Heuber Farmhouse**
A square building floorplan divided into:
*   **Living Room:** The main entrance point (has the front door and trapdoor to the basement).
*   **Peripheral Rooms:** Kitchen, Bedroom, Tool Storage, Pantry, and Brewing Equipment area.
*   **Basement:** Contains the access point for the tunnel leading to the Ant Colony.

**Giant Ant Colony**
A subterranean network represented as a series of connected circles (chambers) linked by narrow lines:
*   **Central Node:** Communal Chamber.
*   **Outlying Chambers:** Queen, 1d6+2 Drones, Eggs, Corpse Pit, Grey Ooze, and two chambers with 1d10/3d10 ants.
*   **Connection:** One line labeled "to farm house."
