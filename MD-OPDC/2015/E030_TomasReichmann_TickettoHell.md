### 2. Text Transcription

#### **Intro**
An industrial revolution transformed Hell into a ruthless corporation. Trains ship souls to various places of torment and suffering. Hell’s minions put on uniforms and exchanged pitchforks for stun batons.
Adventurers who are sentenced to Hell along with other damned souls travel by train and visit locations each embodying one of the 7 deadly sins. Players face challenges which tempt them to commit sins punishable by loss of body parts. Only if they resist temptation, atone for their dark past and embrace divine virtues, do they earn their salvation.

#### **The Seven Stops**

*   **1st Stop – Glutton Hall (Temptation: Gluttony | Redemption: Sharing)**
    “This is the last meal you will ever have. Here, you don’t die from hunger, you wither for all eternity. To remember the taste of food, you feast today. Tomorrow you suffer.”
    Adventurers fight other souls for food. After eating, the devils weigh the party and cut away the corresponding weight in flesh from their bodies. Sharing food allows players to avoid this price.
*   **2nd Stop – Lustful Palace (Temptation: Lust | Redemption: Conscience/Modesty)**
    “I have good news and bad news. You are about to meet some very sexy ladies and gentlemen in this palace. The bad news is, we are going to remove your privates afterwards.”
    Adventurers can exploit charmed prostitutes (risking disease) or investigate a rumored hidden library/crystal. Resisting exploitation keeps them physically whole.
*   **3rd Stop – Valley of Sloth (Temptation: Sloth | Redemption: Hard work)**
    “The tracks won’t clear themselves. Don’t make me use my whip you lousy lot!”
    Tracks are buried in rubble. Souls must clear the debris. The group cannot proceed until everyone contributes their labor.
*   **4th Stop – Trials of Wrath (Temptation: Wrath/Vengeance | Redemption: Mercy)**
    “Another train awaits you on the other side, I wouldn’t stay here if I were you.”
    A series of traps that target anyone except the person who triggered them. The challenge is to survive the wrath of those you hurt and forgive those who hurt you.
*   **5th Stop – The Greedy Wheel Casino (Temptation: Greed | Redemption: Self-control)**
    “Before we stop at Devil’s Envy Market, you might want to take a shot with The Greedy Wheel. I would wish you luck, but you wouldn’t buy it anyway.”
    Classic casino games where the currency is body parts. Devils cheat, but some souls win big.
*   **6th Stop – Devil’s Envy Market (Temptation: Envy | Redemption: Charity)**
    “Here you can spend your hard-earned money. Choose wisely, you will not get another chance for a long long time.”
    Winners from the casino spend gold here. Other souls try to steal from the players. Mercy and sharing gold heals wounds and regrows lost limbs.
*   **7th Stop – Hell’s Pride (Temptation: Pride | Redemption: Humility)**
    “We will be passing Hell’s Pride. This is actually the only way to get out of hell, so you understand we have it well guarded.”
    A fight against an unbeatable guardian. Players will fall and be resurrected with damage repeatedly. Once they grow humble and stop fighting, the path to salvation is revealed.
