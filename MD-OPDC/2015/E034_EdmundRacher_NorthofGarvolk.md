**Introduction:**
The Dukes of Garvolk have long governed this remote province of The Empire, to the north of the great plains and the river and the inland seas. The comparative autonomy of Garvolcia has led to laws restricting the practice of magic and the free movement of the magical peoples. The new Duke, schooled in the cosmopolitan atmosphere of the imperial capital has now, as one of his first edicts, relaxed these opening up a set of opportunities for your band of adventurers with its requisite complement of humanoids, hedge wizards, wandering mystics, scarred mercenaries and self-appointed paragons of virtue.....

#### Adventures in and around....Garvolk
*   **High Drama:** A play mocking religious figures is being performed by a traveling company. The Prioress of St Rhadegunt wants them deterred, while the Count of Hurst wants to see it. Perhaps a private showing at the Ducal seat of Hurst is the answer?
*   **The Long Arm of the Law:** A judge’s daughter was murdered; the culprit is hiding in the Westbank slums. Lawmen will pay for his arrest; the father will pay even more for his death.
*   **The Blue Plumes:** A political faction opposed to the new Duke. They are intimidating newcomers and plotting to assassinate the Castellan of Duke Kasimir’s Tower to launch a coup d’état.

#### Adventures in and around....Kordon
*   **Underground:** The mines are full of flammable gas. You need a non-sparking light source to find a way past the gas; you’ll be rewarded for discovering promising new veins.
*   **A Case of Adultery:** Silver ingots sent to the Imperial Mint are being adulterated with lead. The conspiracy involves miners trying to reclaim control of their guild.

#### Adventures in and around....Market Blackridge
*   **Sheep Mange:** A skin disease is ruining the local wool trade. The mineral springs at Rustwater could kill the parasite, but they will permanently stain the wool. There is no easy cure.
*   **A Matter of Reputation:** A traveling musician wrote a libelous ballad about a wealthy merchant’s wife. Track him down and get an apology.

#### Adventures in and around....Ruddygorge
*   **The Gaggle:** The Bishop of Garvolk is concerned about a cult forming around a "miracle-working" goosegirl. Investigate for heresy.
*   **The Lord of Colley:** The Lord has been kidnapping imperial road workers because he claims he wasn't compensated for the land the new highway crosses. Resolve this without killing the Lord (a cousin of the Duke).

#### Adventures in and around....Walakh
*   **Heritage:** An Elven representative wants access to an ancient wood spirit shrine on Sir Hugh Bessant’s estate. The knight is stubborn and protective of his timber.
*   **Big Game:** Huntsmen are missing near The Red Lodge. If a monster wolf exists, the Warden wants it found for an aristocratic hunt. If it’s just human poachers, find them.
