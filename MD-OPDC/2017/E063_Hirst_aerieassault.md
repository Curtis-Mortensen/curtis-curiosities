### Map Description for Non-Sighted Players

The adventure area is divided into two distinct parts: the **Surface/Approach** and the **Internal Tower**.

**1. The Surface Map (The Approach):**
Imagine a winding path through a rugged, grassy landscape. You begin at point [X], a drop-off point in a field. From here, the path moves past a garden (1) and a massive chain hanging down (2). It then winds through a patrolled area (3) toward a central point (4), which serves as the entrance to the underground structure. Nearby, there is a crossroads (5) featuring a guard post. A path then leads to the interior, indicated by tunnels leading toward rooms 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, and 11. These rooms are connected by tunnels, with room 8 being a dangerous fissure/pit and room 9 being the clean kobold living quarters.

**2. The Internal Tower (Levels 1–4):**
The interior is represented by four distinct floor plans, each marked as a separate "Level" on a grid where 1 square equals 5 feet.

*   **Level 1:** A rectangular floor plan with several rooms. There is a locked chest in the northwest corner, a secret door on the north wall, and a locked door to the southeast that leads to a small room containing a pool of water.
*   **Level 2:** A complex floor plan. To the south is the kitchen (with barrels and the half-orc). To the east is a bedroom with two beds. In the northwest corner of this floor is a small, trapped room (the "Strangling Curtains" room).
*   **Level 3:** This level consists of a central hallway. There is a "trapped floor" (indicated by a diamond symbol ♦) which acts as a chute dropping down to Level 8. In the northeast corner, there is a pillar that acts as a lever/mechanism to open a hatch.
*   **Level 4:** This is a small, single-chamber room. It is the final area where you encounter the doppelganger.

**Navigational Key:**

*   **♦ (Diamond icon):** Indicates a physical trap (the chute).
*   **‡ (Double dagger):** Marks the location of the doppelganger.
*   **♠ (Spade icon):** Indicates the location of the specific silver key mentioned by the half-orc.
*   **† (Dagger):** Indicates a hazard that triggers a random creature encounter (the swarm).
