### Map Description for Navigation
This dungeon is an isometric sewer system divided into two primary zones: the northern "sewer/guard" area and the southern "religious/pastry" area.

1.  **The Northern Entry (Sewer Tunnels):** The party enters from a tunnel at the top-left labeled "Sewer Entrance." This tunnel heads right, passing a homeless NPC, and transitions into a wider walkway featuring a "watch route" patrolled by mercenaries. There is a pit of "contaminated water" that should be avoided.
2.  **The Central Junction:** A large junction point sits in the middle. It features a pipe with "falling sewage" and a "ratmen pantry" that is infested with giant cockroaches.
3.  **The Main Temple (The Core):** A path leads from the junction into the large, central room called the "Ratmen temple of the Albino God." In the center of this room is an elevated, square platform containing the **Albino God** (the giant crocodile). A "wastes waterfall" flows into this room.
4.  **The Religious Chambers:** To the left of the central temple is the "Ratmen common room" (the living quarters). Further off to the side is the storage area for "stocked corpses" and the "altar to the Albino God," which overlooks the main crocodile chamber.
5.  **The Secret/Pastry Area:** To the bottom right of the main temple, a "flooded passage" connects to a "cellar." This cellar is hidden behind crates and leads to "Krock’s delicacies" kitchen. The kitchen is "magically closed" off from the main area. Connected to this are the "High priest Krock chambers," which contain a net meant for collecting the crocodile’s dung for the pies.
