**Introduction:** On most worlds, adventures are strung together with travel like pearls on a necklace. A day’s journey usually begins and ends in a settlement, winding through the hills and dales of the world at large. This One Page Dungeon provides a single day of travel with encounters and points of interest. Although designed for north at the top, it is easy enough to turn the map sideways or even upside-down.
