**Title:** THE QUINTESSENTIAL DUNGEON
**Intro:** Rumours say the long-dead wizard Bargle built this dungeon to guard his treasures. In truth, it's a trial to recruit adventurers to his undying cause!

**Callouts (roughly clockwise from top left):**
*   **Inn/Ropes:** Lured by danger and riches, the party descend on ropes from the Green Griffin Inn - once they've proven their mettle in a barroom brawl!
*   **Chessboard:** Step on the wrong squares and the giant chess pieces stomp forward to attack!
*   **Cellar/Rats:** Alternatively, 'Old Garleb' hires them to kill the rats in his cellar! Gnome wererat 'Whiskers' Gnawl pillages the cellar with his rat swarms. It's a ploy to force Old Garleb to accept protection from the Thieves' Guild!
*   **Sewer Pipe:** A green slime drools on anyone passing through the sewer.
*   **Rug of Smothering:** A rug of smothering protects the treasure gem.
*   **Treasure/Stairs:** Opening the treasure chest causes the stairs to collapse, pitching characters into the otyugh pit! Within the chest is the key for the locked chamber beyond.
*   **Otyugh:** A flatulent otyugh wallows in the sewage.
*   **Statue:** The statue's eye sockets contain the fire and earth gems. But the statue is tall and slippery. Be careful!
*   **Dragon/Troll Area:** Cave trolls camp here, playing knucklebones. They keep a halfling thief Bungo bladders, tied up in a cage. If the dragon wakes, she devours the adventurers unless they impress her with a story.
*   **Mimic:** A mimic posing as a treasure chest atop a pile of glass gems.
*   **Fountain:** The magic mouth here asks a riddle. If answered correctly, it tells them to drink from the fountain to learn the location of the four elemental gems. If they drink before then, the water is poisoned!
*   **Locked Door/Ending:** Four elemental gems are required to open this door. The treasure chest within contains 1,000gp and the legendary Deck of Many Things! If the deck is taken, the dungeon starts to collapse!
*   **Rust Monster Pit:** A rust monster lives in the pit. Guarding the air gem. A wall of force covers the pit. The platforms trigger teleportation effects when stood upon: A,B,D: Teleport creatures standing on the opposite platform into the pit. C: Teleport creatures in the pit into the room.
*   **Magic Mirror:** The magic mirror reverses the viewer's alignment.
*   **Banquet Hall:** Feast here to regain health, but beware the enchanted grog! (Random potion effect).
*   **River:** A fast-flowing, icy cold river!
*   **Kobold Bridge:** Crafty kobolds hurl fire bombs at those who dare cross their holy bridge!
*   **Bottomless Pit:** Beware! Bottomless pit!

**Outro:** ...If the adventurers return to the inn with the deck of many things, Old Garleb reveals himself as the wizard Bargle! He has a quest for them... but that's a story for another day!
