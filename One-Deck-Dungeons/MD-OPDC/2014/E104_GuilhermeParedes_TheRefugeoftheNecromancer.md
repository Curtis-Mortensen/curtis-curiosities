**Header**
**The Refuge of the Necromancer**
A One-Page-Dungeon for beginner heroes
Recommended: 1º – 2º Level / System: any

**Backgrounds**
A terrified Citadel hires the heroes after many missing persons. The last person is a 14-year-old daughter of a merchant. A Noble offers 400gp as a reward to those who solve the problem. Following the clues given by a hunter nearby, the heroes arrive at the doors of an ancient temple in ruins abandoned long ago.

**The Villain**
Rasfith is a follower of the necromancy arts. He plans to create a force of undead to dominate the Citadel. He made the secret rooms of the ancient temple his own domain. It is he who is capturing people to be his undead servants.

**1 – Great Hall of Ancient Temple**
There are 3 ferocious giant rats per hero. They are eating the remains of a corpse in the middle of the room and attack the heroes. There are ruins of ancient construction and wreckage of chairs. In the pulpit is the key to room 10 in a secret compartment.

**2 – Floor Trap**
In the middle of this passage, there is a hidden 10-foot deep pit.

**3 – Old Library**
There is 1 zombie per hero, guarding this room. On the north wall there is a secret passage leading to the left of the dungeon. There is a chest in the corner with a splash of acid trap. Inside the chest is: 2 common ancient medallions and 1 vial of healing potion. On the shelves, many old books and scrolls—unimportant.

**4 / 7 / 9 – Access Pass**
Only serve to shift to other rooms.

**5 – Altar of the Skull**
In this room there is a skull that exudes a crackling light. Playing with the skull causes the hero to lose 1 level. Destroying the skull causes a burst of energy that hurts all nearby.

**6 – Mummy King**
This room is locked from the outside with bars. The mummy inside his sarcophagus will not attack if not harassed. Inside the sarcophagus is 1 ruby, 5 emeralds, and a scepter with precious stones.

**8 – Skeletons in the Armorial**
In this room there are 2 skeletons armed with swords and spears for each hero guarding this place. There is an armorial with more weapons.

**10 – Altar of Doom**
In this room there is a table. Rasfith sacrifices people from the Citadel here to make them undead. At this time, a young girl, probably the daughter of the merchant, is to be sacrificed. The light in this room is dismal and there is an altar to an unholy God. In a chest is 1 cursed dagger, 1 magic scroll of Fireball, 1 magic scroll of Create Undead, 200gp. On a shelf, 1 book on human anatomy, 1 atom of a God of the Dead, 1 spellbook with spells: Invisibility, Magic Missile, Read Magic, Undead Command, and Create Undead. Rasfith will try to ambush the heroes if he realizes they are coming. He will flee the stairs if losing the fight. The key to the door and the chest is on a chain around Rasfith’s neck. The stairs lead out of the dungeon by a secret way.

### Map Description (for Play)
The dungeon is a small, linear complex of rooms connected by narrow hallways, based on a 5-foot grid.

*   **The Entrance (Room 1):** You begin at the bottom center of the map in a large, rectangular room. This is the "Great Hall."
*   **The Hallway (Room 2):** From the center of the north wall of the Great Hall, a long, narrow corridor leads straight north. This corridor contains a floor trap (the pit).
*   **The Junction:** The corridor ends at a junction. 
    *   To the **West** is the **Old Library (Room 3)**. 
    *   To the **East** is the **Altar of the Skull (Room 5)**.
    *   Continuing **North** from the junction leads into **Access Pass (Room 4)**.
*   **The Upper Levels:**
    *   From **Room 4**, move north into the **Mummy King’s room (Room 6)**. 
    *   From **Room 6**, a passage leads north to another **Access Pass (Room 7)**.
    *   From **Room 7**, you move north into the **Armorial (Room 8)**.
    *   From the **Armorial (Room 8)**, a hallway leads west into the **Access Pass (Room 9)**.
*   **The Final Chamber (Room 10):** The final room is in the top-left corner of the map. It connects directly to **Room 9** via a door. This room contains the stairs leading out of the dungeon.

**Key Features:**
*   **Locked Doors (L):** Found leading into the Mummy King's chamber (Room 6) and the final Altar (Room 10).
*   **Secret Doors (S):** One connects the library (Room 3) to the far-left corridor (Room 9/10).
*   **Layout Note:** Most paths are narrow hallways (5 feet wide), while the rooms are generally 15x15 or 20x20 squares.
