There is a "Cave Barracks" tucked into the southwest corner, while "Stores" are located in the northwest.
*   **Topography:** To the north and west, there are "Silt Lands" and a "Mooring" area, implying this location is near a waterway or coastline. A "Hillside Path" winds down from the top of the map, leading toward the underground sections. A "Creek" flows down from the north into the courtyard area.

*   **The Subterranean Caverns:** Once inside the tunnel, you descend into a lower level that opens up into a large, dark cavern system.

    *   **The Eel Lair:** Also on the eastern side, there is a series of interconnected pools where the Land Eels are bred. These are clearly marked as a progression of danger (from tadpoles to trainable eels).
    *   **The Worship Fountain:** At the very bottom center of the map is the "Ancient Fountain Worship Room." This is a cramped, dangerous chamber dominated by a large, monstrous fountain featuring the "30' Long Land Eel." The text warns that the hidden passage through the fountain's mouth is a major access point to further secrets.
