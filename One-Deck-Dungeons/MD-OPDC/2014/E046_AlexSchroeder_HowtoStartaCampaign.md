**Faction Headquarters**

**1. Wizard Tower**
A magic mouth greets visitors and asks their name and business. The first floor is guarded by 5 animated objects (2 HD) at the command of the magic mouth. Top floor also houses a talking, mean crocodile.
*Map Notes:* Open window above the door. The magic mouth will alert inhabitants if trespassers are seen. It never sleeps. Love potions and stuffed animals can be sold for 400 gp.

**2. Thieves’ Den**
The front room is a fake taylor’s shop with an old man doing needlework. He points to the right and locks the door if any pass into the trapped room: one big pit (1d6). Members ignore the nod and go straight through. They know the secret doors (through hearth). Big room in the back has a chest with 500 gp. 10 thieves hang out and watch the doors. Secret door to the sewers in the back.

**3. Bakery**
Starting a fight in the bakery attracts a lot of hungry looters in 10 rds. 6 apprentices work here, armed with clubs (F1). They use flour to blind intruders (-4), save to avoid. 
*Contents:* Big bags of flour, two ovens, piles of fire wood, buckets of water, salt, a large table, wooden tools to use as clubs. In the cellar, pots of yeast, blankets, some extra clothes, and the owner’s chest with 200 gp in it.

### Map Descriptions for Accessibility

#### 1. The Wizard Tower Map
This map consists of two circular floor plans, stacked vertically.
*   **The Bottom Level:** A large circle representing the ground floor. A smaller circular projection is attached to the bottom-left of the main circle, representing the entrance/foyer where the Magic Mouth is located. An "up" arrow indicates the path to the next level.
*   **The Top Level:** Another circle of similar size positioned directly above the bottom one. A small rectangular notch on the top edge represents a balcony or open window.

#### 2. The Thieves’ Den Map
This is a dungeon-style grid map, roughly 9x9 squares in size.
*   **The Layout:** The entrance is at the top left. The floor plan winds through several interconnected rooms. The "fake tailor shop" is the top-left area. 
*   **The Trap:** A square room immediately to the right of the entrance contains a marked pit trap (indicated by a cross-hatch pattern).
*   **The Hearth:** A small, specialized room in the center contains a hearth, which serves as the hub for secret doors.
*   **The Back Room:** A large, open space in the bottom right corner of the grid, marked with a chest icon. 
*   **Sewer Exit:** A final passage leads off the bottom right of the main room, representing the secret sewer exit.

#### 3. The Bakery Map
This is a two-part map, showing a ground floor and a cellar.
*   **The Ground Floor:** A large square room (6x6 grid). The top-left corner features the entrance. The floor is mostly open space, with internal markers for ovens and tables.
*   **The Cellar:** Below the ground floor is a smaller square (4x4 grid). A "down" arrow connects the two. The cellar is a compact storage space for the chest, yeast, and blankets mentioned in the description.
