**THE BLACK SCHOOL**
*A One Page Dungeon by Fco. Javier Barrera*

**THE LEGEND OF THE BLACK SCHOOL**
"North of the city walls of Ur-Agnès, beyond the opalescent mansions of its princes, you will find the ruins of a school which was called the Black School. It was a school below ground, eternally dark and changeless, where you could learn a thorough and perfect knowledge of the Old Arts. It was pretty well known among the pupils that the devil himself was the master. The legend says that one of the rules of the school was that the owner should keep for himself that one of the students who would leave the school the last every year."

The truth behind the legend is that the Black School existed but its real name was “School of Old and Modern Arts”, and was founded by Onagro the Reluctant. The prestige of Onagro and the possibility to gain access to his legendary library attracted the best minds of that age, and the prominent noble families of the Empire sent their children to study in there.

However, the unfortunate death in strange circumstances of the governor’s first-born triggered off a succession of terrible events that finished with the destruction of the School and the disappearance of Onagro.

**RUMOURS (D4)**
*   An elf prince is negotiating with the governor to settle with his people in the burial mounds zone, North of the city. He states that there rest the remains of his noble ancestors.
*   Onagro was in fact a powerful necromancer. He accepted young students to murder them and use their blood and their fat in horrific rituals of Necromancy.
*   Onagro was a victim of his disciples’ ambitions. They longed for his knowledge and tried to get rid of him. They testified falsely against him when the governor’s first-born died by accident in a magical experiment. However, they did not measure well the consequences of their accusation. The School was assaulted and destroyed, Onagro disappeared and was given up for dead and his library was lost forever.
*   It is said that the magnificent library of the Black School housed a scroll handwritten by the gods in which he locked up the words that gave rise to our world.

**THE ELVES (ENCOUNTER)**
Dalenla Ingle and his guards have set up their camp near the ruins of the School. Helped by a powerful enchantment, he has managed to open a way safe between the rubbles and the rocks that blocked the entry and now he is about to enter in search of the famous library of Onagro. He will mistrust the adventurers, but he will try to reach an agreement instead of fighting the adventurers.

*Dalenla Ingle (Elf fighter-mage; NPC; Powerful).* Ring of invisibility, Armor of Peacefulness (Cursed armor. Every time Dalenla kills an enemy, he must save against spells or he will die. To remove the curse is his main motivation to enter the Black School and find the Library) and other magical equipment. Elven mercenaries and Grel (charmed ghoul; NPC; Weak. Used as an undead energy detector).

**MAP KEY**

1. **ENTRANCE.** It’s absolute darkness. There is no more than dust and debris accumulated by the passing of time.
2. **ROOM OF THE CARYATIDS.** Onagro built the School over a Sochara mausoleum. In the North wall of the room a secret door leads to the mausoleum, but only will be revealed pronouncing some specific words. The Caryatids that delimits the door are its guardians. Once the secret door is revealed, the caryatids will come alive and attack the intruders. Only will be stopped with a specific command word: *Caryatid Colum.*
3. **LAVATORY.** In the center of the room there is a fountain built in ornamented stone. Now it is dry, but it’s magical, and fresh water will spring up if the word “Frescor” is pronounced. The word is carved in the stone of the fountain.
4. **SUPPLIES.** Empty boxes and barrels.
5. **ROOMS.** Guest rooms. Nothing special.
6. **LIBRARY.** Piles of books block the door from the inside. The library has been practically destroyed by the passing of time and the dementia of Onagro. A skeleton of a small animal lies on the ground. There’s a ruby beside the skeleton that contains the soul of Onagro’s familiar. If someone swallows the ruby, the spirit of the familiar will take possession of his body.
6b. **STUDY ROOM.** Onagro the Reluctant is here, now turned into a Gray Philosopher, reading eternally the same books... He found in the old books the words needed to reveal the secret door of the mausoleum... Whatever he found in the mausoleum terrified him, and returned here to die alone of starvation in darkness.
*Onagro (Gray Philosopher; Powerful NPC).* He keeps with him a Sochara scroll... he wrote it bad [the passwords] for whether anybody could snatch him the parchment. (True password for the mausoleum is “Dark Crystal”).
7. **SPELL CASTER’S LABORATORY.** There are 500 g.c. in magical components scattered on the ground. In the West wall there’s a secret wardrobe with more magical components (5,000 g.c.), 6 gems (700 g.c.), some mage scrolls and a Sapient Sword named Quimérica.
8. **ALCHEMIST’S LABORATORY.** 500 g.c. in magical components. Alchemist’s tools (1,000 g.c.).
9. **THE MAUSOLEUM OF GAARTHEE’A.** This is the entrance hall of the Mausoleum... [contains] a hundred of mummified human slaves... At the back... the statue of a large bloated creature... those who approach... will listen how a profane canticle starts to ascend... Astral searchers [will appear]. Behind the statue, in the North wall, a second secret door is hidden. It is protected by a mortal trap.

### 3. Map Description (for navigation)

The dungeon is laid out as a cluster of rooms built into the earth, with a North-facing orientation.

*   **Entrance (1):** A small square chamber at the southeast corner of the map.
*   **Corridor:** A long hallway runs from the Entrance (1) northwards.
*   **The Southern Cluster:**
    *   **Room of the Caryatids (2):** Directly north of the entrance. It has a secret door (marked "S") on its North wall leading to the Mausoleum.
    *   **Lavatory (3):** Located to the west of the Room of the Caryatids.
    *   **Supplies (4):** A small square room directly south of the Lavatory.
*   **The Western/Central Cluster:**
    *   **Rooms (5):** A series of four small guest chambers grouped together, accessed by a hallway branching off to the west.
    *   **Library (6) & Study (6b):** A large rectangular area on the west side of the complex. The Study (6b) is a small room separated from the main library area by a doorway.
*   **The Eastern/Northern Cluster:**
    *   **Spell Caster’s Lab (7):** A large room on the east side, north of the Entrance.
    *   **Alchemist’s Lab (8):** A smaller room adjacent to the Spell Caster's Lab.
*   **The Mausoleum (9):** This is a large, rectangular chamber that takes up the entire northern portion of the map. It is isolated from the rest of the school, accessible only through the secret door in Room 2. There is a "T" (trap) symbol marked near the northern wall of this room, indicating a hidden passage behind the statue.
