### Map Description for Players
The map is a horizontal, cross-section schematic of the *SS Hecate Rose*. Think of it as a long, narrow tube oriented left-to-right.

*   **The Rear (Left Side):** This is where your team enters. The **Hangar Deck** is at the very rear. Attached to the rear-sides are two **Engine Nacelles** (the propulsion units). Just forward of the hangar, the **Damage Control Locker** and **Engineering/Life Support** are located.
*   **The Midsection:** A central corridor, labeled the **Central Passage**, runs through the middle of the ship, acting as the main artery. Branching off this corridor (going from back to front) are the **Crew Quarters**, the **Galley**, and the **Stateroom**. 
*   **The Front (Right Side):** The ship narrows as it reaches the front. You’ll find the **Captain’s Quarters** and, at the very nose of the ship, the **Bridge**. 
*   **The Sensor Array:** Extending from the very front/top of the ship is the **Comms / Sensor Tower**, which is a distinct, vertical protrusion that looks a bit like a mast or antenna array on a traditional ship. 

The overall layout is linear—you effectively move from the "tail" (Hangar) through the "guts" (Central Passage/Engineering) to reach the "head" (Bridge/Comms).
