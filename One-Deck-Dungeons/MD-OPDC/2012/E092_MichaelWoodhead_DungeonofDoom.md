**DM's INFO:**
Ravaged by a debilitating illness, a half-mad 5th level wizard named Arkimedes can only remember how to teleport, and has created what he likes to call the Dungeon of Doom. He challenges various adventurers passing through to complete the obstacle course he's created in return for wealthy prizes. However, what starts out as a simple test of the PCs' skills soon turns into a deadly competition that could very well lead to their deaths.

**GOAL:**
The primary goal of the PCs is to escape from the Dungeon of Doom alive.

Arkimedes proclaims, "Then you'll never get out of here alive!", and then teleports away. He sends wandering monsters to each room the PCs subsequently enter in their quest to escape.

### Map Description
The map is a series of rooms connected by narrow corridors, laid out on a grid. To visualize it, imagine the flow of the adventure:

*   **The Entrance (1):** Located in the top-left (Northwest) corner. From here, you can go North through the portcullis (exit), East through a secret door, or West into the main complex (Room 2).
*   **The Hub (2):** Directly south of the entrance. This is the central connection point for the "Test Your Mettle" challenge.
*   **The Challenges:** 
    *   From the Hub (2), you go East to the **Archery Contest (3)**, which is a long, narrow rectangle stretching horizontally.
    *   Alternatively, from the Hub (2), you head South to the **Axe-Throwing Contest (4)**, then further South to the **Hold Your Breath Contest (5)**.
    *   From Room 5, the path goes East to the **Swimming Contest (6)**, then further East to the **Mirror Room (7)**.
*   **The Pit and Treasure:** 
    *   East of the Mirror Room (7) is a corridor leading to the **Pit of Peril (8)**, a large square chamber in the middle-right of the map.
    *   Directly south of the Pit (8) is the **Treasure Room (9)**.
*   **The Final Stretch:**
    *   West of the Treasure Room (9) is an **Empty Room (10)**.
    *   South of Room 10 is a corridor leading into a **Water Trap Corridor (11)**.
    *   From Corridor 11, you move East to **Room 12 (Mummies)**.
    *   Finally, East of the Mummies is the **Corridor (13)**, which leads toward the final escape point of the dungeon.

The map is highly linear, forcing players to move from one challenge room to the next in a specific sequence, with the danger increasing as they move deeper toward the East and South sections of the map.
