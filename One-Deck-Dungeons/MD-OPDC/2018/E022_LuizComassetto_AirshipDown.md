### Map Description for Gameplay
The map is an isometric "cutaway" view of a subterranean cave system integrated into the wreckage of an airship. You can think of the layout as a central hub with several branching chambers:

*   **The Northern Entrance (1 & 2):** At the top-left of the map, the broken tail (stern) of the airship sticks out of the ground, serving as the main entrance. This leads into a central T-shaped hallway where the Zombified Crew members patrol.
*   **The Eastern Wing (3):** To the right of the central hallway, there is a natural cavern branch housing the Giant Spiders. A piece of the airship's bow is lodged here, providing a hiding spot for a lone survivor.
*   **The Underwater Area (4):** To the far left, a lower-level cave segment contains a pool of water. At the bottom of this pool rests the treasure chest, guarded by Merfolk.
*   **The Central Hub (5):** The heart of the dungeon is a large, square-shaped cargo hold. It contains stacked wooden crates and serves as the main patrol area for the Lizardfolk Warriors.
*   **The Western Wing (6):** Accessible from the central hold, this is a caged area sectioned off as a prison, where the captured crew is being held by a Lizardman guard.
*   **The Elevated Eastern Wing (7 & 8):** On the right side of the map, stone staircases lead *up* to higher platforms.
    *   **The Shaman's Lair (7)** is on the upper-right plateau, featuring tables and alchemical equipment.
    *   **The Throne Room (8)** is the highest point, located on a raised stone dais at the bottom-right, where the Lizard King sits on a throne, looking out over the rest of the cave.

The map connects these areas with natural stone walls, rocky floors, and broken segments of the airship’s wooden hull, creating a mix of claustrophobic corridors and open chambers.
