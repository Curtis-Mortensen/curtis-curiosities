### Map Description for Gameplay
The map is a vertical cross-section of a tall, narrow stone tower, which is effectively a 4-story dungeon cylinder.

*   **Floor 1 (Base):** This is a square room on the ground floor. The layout shows a desk/table (d) in the center with shelves (a) along the walls. A terrarium (b) is tucked into a corner, and the brass levers (c) are positioned on a wall. There is a clear trapdoor beneath the furniture.
*   **Floor 2 (Mid-section):** A circular room filled with a large circular pool of acid (e) in the center. Four skeletons (f) are suspended by chains from the ceiling above the pool. The walls show winch mechanisms for the levers (g).
*   **Floor 3 (Top section):** A room with a large statue (j) in the center holding the "Blood Egg." There are stained glass windows (i) on the curved walls, a hammock (l) hung from the ceiling, and a crate (k). Above the statue is a large skylight (h).
*   **Floor 4 (Roof):** This is the very top of the tower. When the trap triggers, the entire structure flips vertically. 
    *   **Gameplay note:** When describing this to players, emphasize the **verticality**. Use the pipe on the side of the tower as a point of reference for fluid movement (acid).
