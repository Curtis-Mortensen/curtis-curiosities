**THE GLAMOURHAMMER (GLAM-ER-HAM-ER)**
*   A very lonely 8’ mutant mantis shrimp. Likes to be by himself, but hates being alone.
*   **The Hammer:** The fearful pistol punch. Claw-hammer that hits at over 50 mph. Creates momentary shock bubbles at ~8,500 F (4,700 C).

*   **Status:** Trapped, bored, & angry. A restless god-shrimp. Mutated by a cruel experiment, broke free. Detached from purpose, home, or a hope for normalcy.

### 3. Map Layout for Gameplay
To run this map, visualize it as a flow chart of interconnected rooms:

1.  **Entrance:** You start at the surface and descend a rope ladder to **"The Landing"** (contains bones/swords). A door leads South.
2.  **The First Hub:** Through the door, you find the **"Entry"** (1d3 friendly cultists). From this room, paths radiate out based on the doors described:
    *   **South:** Leads to the **"Library"** (chandeliers, books) and the **"Long Walk Down"** (a 20’ slope).
    *   **East/West/South:** Various doors lead to the **"Arguing Cultists"** room, **"Nulops’ Quarters,"** the **"Kitchen,"** and the **"Shrine"** (where a secret door lies under an altar).
3.  **The Lower Level:** Following the "Long Walk Down" or the "Shrine" path eventually brings you to the **"Shoreline."**
4.  **The Boss Room:** From the Shoreline, you must pass through to the **"Lair"** where the Glamourhammer is located. This area is under the water line and is distinctively dangerous ("lungs will collapse if too close").

**Navigational Notes for the DM:**
*   **Signs:** No one speaks; everyone communicates via hand signs.
*   **Sound:** The "clicks" increase in intensity the deeper the players go.
*   **Lighting:** Some areas (indicated by dashed lines on the map) are poorly lit or pitch black, which impacts stealth and combat visibility.
*   **Flow:** The map is designed to be traversed linearly at first, then branching out into the "cult living quarters" before funneling back down into the final Lair.
