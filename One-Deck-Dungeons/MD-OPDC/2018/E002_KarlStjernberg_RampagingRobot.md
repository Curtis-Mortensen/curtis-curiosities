### Map Description for Non-Visual Play

**The Hex Map:**
The hex map is a 2x2 grid labeled A, B, C, D. 
*   **Hex A** (Top-Left): Home Base (The Airfield).
*   **Hex B** (Top-Right): Overgrown Highway (Jungle).
*   **Hex C** (Bottom-Left): City of Night (Ruins).
*   **Hex D** (Bottom-Right): The Lair of the Steel Crawler. This is the goal.

**The Steel Crawler (Exterior View):**
The giant robot is depicted as a massive, hunched, mechanical beast made of various scrap parts. It has several distinct sections:
*   **The Head (Top):** Contains the Crow’s Nest (5) and the winch/crane.
*   **Mid-Section:** Contains the Penthouse rooms (A1, A2, A3).
*   **Upper Body:** Contains the Crew Quarters (B1, B2, B3).
*   **Lower Body/Hips:** Contains the Metal Innards (C1, C2, C3, C4) and the Death-Spitter turret (2).
*   **Bottom/Feet:** The Slime Pit (D1) at the base.

**Interior Floor Maps:**
These are individual circular or squared-off floor plans showing how the internal rooms connect:
1.  **Penthouse (A1-A3):** A triangular layout. The entrance (A1) leads to a kennel (A2) and a loot hoard (A3). 
2.  **Crew Quarters (B1-B3):** A linear path. You enter through a walkway (B1), which leads to a large sleeping room (B2) and a drop-down hatch (B3).
3.  **Metal Innards (C1-C4):** A more technical, squared-off layout. C1 is the access room. C2 is the battery room. C3 is the gunner's station. C4 is the control bridge where the boss (Gathoraxx) is located.
4.  **Slime Pit (D1):** A single, circular chamber at the very bottom of the structure, isolated from the others except via the hole in the floor from C1.
