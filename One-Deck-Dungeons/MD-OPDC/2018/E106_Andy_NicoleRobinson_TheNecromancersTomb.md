**Header**
*   **Title:** The Necromancer’s Tomb
*   **Credits:** by Andy & Nicole Robinson
*   **Introduction:** Centuries ago, The Necromancer was defeated in his laboratory. The entrance was sealed with his surviving minions still inside. It is now his Tomb. Recent earthquakes have opened a crack in the cliff face. High Priest Nezbit is concerned that the new passages may connect to the laboratory. More importantly, there may be powerful magics left inside and unguarded. Please retrieve any items of power so that the Temple can guard or destroy them.

**Room & Encounter Descriptions**
*   **1. Tomb Entrance:** Powerful enchantments have sealed this entrance and they are still intact.
*   **2. Earthquakes:** Earthquakes have opened this natural tunnel.
*   **3. The Entry Room:** One corner of the room has caved in and connects to the outside via the new tunnel. The room is occupied by 3 skeletons. One of the skeletons has a bronze medallion (A) around its neck. Combat will attract the attention of the skeletons in the adjacent chamber.
*   **4. The Library:** Dusty tomes of lore, alchemy, necromancy and magic line the shelves of this room. There are 4 Skeletons in the room. They will wander into area 3 if they hear combat. Searching the books thoroughly will find the key to the puzzle door in area 6 as well as another bronze medallion (B) with an arcane symbol on it.
*   **5. False door:** Attempting to open this door activates a pit trap. The pit is 10 foot deep and anyone falling into in suffers 1d6 falling damage. There is another bronze medallion (C) with an arcane symbol on it in the center of the door.
*   **6. Puzzle door:** A magically locked door with 3 bronze circles on the upper part. To open the door the 3 bronze medallions need to be put into the correct spots on the door. Attempting to open the door without all the medallions in the correct spots causes fire to arc out from the door in a 5 foot wide line directly in front of the door causing 1D6 fire damage to anyone in its path.
*   **7. Another puzzle door:** It is magically locked and will only open when the puzzle is completed correctly. Two medallions are embedded in the door to the Library – one represents Fire the other Ice. Written on the far door are two riddles and two spots for medallions. “When the son of the water returns to the parent, it dies. What is it?” and “I eat, I live. I breathe, I live. I drink, I die. What am I?” The answer to the first riddle is Ice and the second riddle is Fire. Placing a medallion in the incorrect spot results in 1d6 ice damage or 1d6 fire damage, depending on which medallion was used.
*   **8. Laboratory:** A wraith (formerly The Necromancer) attacks any intruders. He has a Rod of Undead Control. His workbench has a valuable spell book and a handful of gems are scattered about (a half finished phylactery).
*   **9. Ambush:** On their way back out, the party is ambushed by High Priest Nezbit, who wants the Rod for himself.
*   **10. Passage to nowhere:** An ooze or pudding monster might lurk here should the adventurers still be craving danger.

### Map Description for Navigation
If you were exploring this map, here is the layout:

*   **The Entrance:** You begin at the top of the map, entering through a jagged crack in the cliff face marked as **Area 2**.
*   **The First Room (Area 3):** Follow the tunnel south to enter a rectangular room. This room has a cave-in in the northeast corner (where you entered). Three skeletons are located here.
*   **The Library (Area 4):** Directly south of the entry room is a larger, central rectangular room filled with bookshelves. This is the heart of the dungeon.
*   **The Pit Trap (Area 5):** If you exit the Library to the east, you find a small dead-end corridor with a door marked as a "False door." There is a pit trap immediately in front of it.
*   **The Puzzle Sequence:**
    *   Moving south from the Library, you encounter the first **Puzzle Door (Area 6)**. 
    *   Continuing south from there, you enter a small transition corridor leading to a **Second Puzzle Door (Area 7)**.
*   **The Laboratory (Area 8):** Once you pass the second puzzle door, you enter the final room at the bottom of the map, which is the Necromancer's Laboratory.
*   **The Ambush Path:** If you head back out through the tunnel on the far right side of the map (extending from the top of the Library), you will pass through **Area 9**, where you are ambushed by the High Priest.
*   **The Dead End (Area 10):** Tucked away in the far top-right of the complex, there is a narrow, natural tunnel branching off from the main path, which leads to a "dead end" where a monster might be hiding.
