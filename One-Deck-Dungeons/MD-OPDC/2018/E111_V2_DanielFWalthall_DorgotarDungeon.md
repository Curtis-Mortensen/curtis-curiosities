**Story Hook**
Local Merchants Guild is paying 40 gp (per PC) to clear out all the Goblins from Dorgotar. It will take 3 days travel to reach the entrance.

**Story Twist**
The Goblins recently discovered a natural cave system that is connected to the dungeon. A hungry Rust Monster lurks within the dark caverns! Also, some Bandits will arrive at the dungeon soon, with plans to slay the Goblins and carry off their loot.

### Map Description (for the visually impaired)

The map is divided into two distinct zones: the structured "Dungeon" (top/left) and the natural "Caverns" (bottom/right).

**The Dungeon (Stone Rooms 1-6):**
The entrance is located on the far left side of the map, leading directly into **Room 1**, which acts as the central hub. 
*   **Room 1** is a central square room with a circular fountain in the middle. 
*   Directly **North** of Room 1 is **Room 2**.
*   **North-East** of Room 1 is **Room 3**, which has a secret door on its north wall.
*   **East** of Room 1 is a corridor that leads to **Room 4** (the altar room).
*   **South-East** of Room 1 is **Room 5**, which also contains a secret door.
*   **West** of Room 1 is a path that leads to **Room 6**. This room is notable because its southwest corner has collapsed, creating a jagged opening that serves as the doorway into the caverns.

**The Caverns (Natural Rooms 7-8):**
Passing through the collapsed wall in Room 6 leads you into a natural, winding tunnel system.
*   **Room 7** is a winding passage that flows southward. It is wide and irregular in shape.
*   The path expands into **Room 8**, the final and largest chamber. This room is a massive cavern dominated by a deep chasm running north-to-south. A narrow natural stone bridge crosses this chasm, connecting the room's entrance to a large, flat clearing on the far side. 

**Navigation Summary:**
If you imagine the map like a clock face, the dungeon entrance is at 9 o'clock. You move through a central cluster of rooms (1-6) before exiting the "constructed" part of the map at approximately 7 or 8 o'clock, which drops you into the winding natural tunnels (7-8) that end at the large cavern at the bottom right of the map.
