**Introduction**
The River Gorge Gang has claimed this narrow waterway as their own, and now demand an extortion toll for anyone wishing to pass. Downriver are Redshank's Runners, a band of smugglers who have made a deal with the gang for unfettered passage. Baron Lusario, high society grifter and charismatic narcissist, has it in for the smugglers after they out-scammed him in a crooked transaction. He has put a hefty bounty out for the capture of their leader, Captain Redshank. Most of the River Gorge Gang are an uncultured, vulgar lot, but this cannot be said of their clever and erudite leader, Naz. While his thugs employ unsophisticated methods of combat and capture, Naz looks for cunning and unconventional ways to get the upper hand. This scenario can be inserted into any journey down a small river.

### Map Description for Navigation
The map depicts a river gorge running through a mountainous area. The main feature is a river that enters from the bottom-left corner and flows northeast toward the top-right corner, indicated by flow lines and "Up/Down" markers.

*   **The Gorge (Exterior):** The river curves gently. Near the bottom-left, the route starts at Area 1. Just after this, a raised platform (Area 2) sits on the bank, overlooking the river. Further along the bank is a dock area (Area 3) with rowboats. A rope bridge (Area 5) spans the gorge overhead.
*   **The Cave System:** The cavern entrance is at Area 4 (a sandy bank). From here, the cave branches into a series of interconnected chambers:
    *   **Entrance:** A tunnel starts at Area 4 and leads to a central intersection at Area 6 and 7.
    *   **Northern Section:** A tunnel heads north from the intersection to the stable (Area 9) and the beast master’s room (Area 10). 
    *   **The "Shitter" and Dining:** Moving east from the central intersection, there is a bridge (Area 11) that leads to a jutting wooden platform over the gorge (Area 12—The Shitter).
    *   **The Living Quarters:** A large, central, somewhat L-shaped room (Area 13) acts as the dining and gambling hall. It connects to the storage room (Area 14) and the sleeping quarters (Area 15).
    *   **The Leader's Lair:** Tucked away in the far bottom-right are the private quarters of the leader, Naz (Area 16), which contains a secret tunnel and connects to the magically locked treasure room (Area 17) in the bottom-most corner of the cavern complex. 
*   **Orientation:** The map is dense with curved, organic lines representing the rock walls of the cave, and jagged, sharp lines representing the steep cliffs of the river gorge. The cave system is roughly circular in layout with the main living and utility rooms concentrated in the middle and the leader’s chambers isolated at the deepest point of the map.
