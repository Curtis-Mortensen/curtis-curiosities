**Tactical Considerations:**
*   **Paths:** The dungeon flows like a maze. A player entering from the top will have to navigate through the central hub to reach the bottom rooms.
*   **Chokepoints:** The hallways connecting the rooms are largely 1-square wide, making them ideal locations for monsters to block progress or for adventurers to hold a defensive line.
*   **Scale:** Because the entire map is a grid, you can treat each square as 5 feet of movement (standard D&D scale). The rooms offer enough space for 2–4 standard-sized miniatures per room, depending on the size of the room.
