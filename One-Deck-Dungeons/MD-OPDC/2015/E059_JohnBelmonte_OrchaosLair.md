**Intro**
Orchoas the Orc leader of the savage Dark Hill Clan has been taken control of by a parasite (created by the chaotic gods) that entered his mouth in a drink drawn from the water in his new lair (Once a Dwarven Outpost they fell to the parasite and claimed by Orchoas when he found it). The parasite latches onto his tongue; it releases a venom that numbs the tongue, slowly drains the blood, and replaces the dead tongue as it grows. Once merged with the body, it begins to influence the host till it becomes the dominant force and causes as much chaos as it can. This has driven Orchoas insane, as the parasite tries to take over and he is acting more fierce and reckless than an average orc.

The walls and floors of these rooms are well-crafted carved stone of dwarven craftsmanship, except for the hall and area 9; these are what’s left of the original cave system the dwarfs didn’t finish this area before the problem. This is what the orcs are using for drinking water. All doors except room 6 are wooden.

1. **Great Hall:** 4 statues of Dwarven warriors are in the corners of this room with 4 large pillars in the center. The statues are headless now (the heads are at the bottom of the pit in area 10). 4 Orcs are in the center of the room guarding the area. A heavy wooden door leads to area 7.
2. **Chapel:** Chapel of a Dwarven god. The statues here are broken, and over them hang 2 banners (top banner: the god of chaos; bottom one: the orc war banner).
3. **Shaman's Room:** This room is now the residence of the Orc Shaman. If a fight takes place in area 1, he has a 50% chance of overhearing it and will join the battle. This room contains 1d4 potions.
4. **Storage:** The orcs use this room to store supplies (rotten food), and some rope and other basic items can be found here.
5. **Orc Barracks:** 6 male orcs are sleeping here. If alerted by one of the orcs in room 3, they will grab their weapons and join the fight; they are too tired and won't hear the battle otherwise.
6. **Dwarven Trouser Room:** This room has a heavy iron door and a complicated lock. The orcs have been trying to break it and have caused it to become stuck. If it is unlocked, it will require some tools such as a crowbar and levers to move it. (Add loot fitting your campaign here and any special items).
7. **Hallway:** This large hall leads to the rough caves (9) and (10) where the females and young are. 3 orcs patrol this area.
8. **Feasting Hall:** This large room, once the dwarven feasting hall, is now home to Orchoas and the 8 orc females and 10 young. This is also where meals are prepared; there is a small cooking area in the bottom left with a smoke stack carved into the ceiling. There is a chest here containing 50 silver.
9. **Pool:** Orchoas is near the pool at area 9. The parasite is controlling his body while he sleeps; it is getting ready to lay its eggs in the water. Treat Orchoas as an orc chief, and if he becomes low on health, the parasite will force control of him and try to kiss the nearest PC and lay its eggs in their mouth. He carries a +2 weapon and a +1 shield.
10. **Pit:** This is a large, cavernous hole that drops over 75 feet. The orcs have not gone down to check it. The dwarfs once had a rope system leading down; traces of the anchors can be seen by a PC checking over the side.
