**Header/Intro:**
*   **Title:** The Burned Library
*   **Subtitle:** A single page dungeon by Vincent Bettenfeld | https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/
*   **Intro:** The library was thought lost in the great eruption of a nearby volcano, a century from now. Local folks now better: it was in fact buried in volcanic ashes, but its content may still be preserved. Any scholar could easily convince the adventurers of how rare, thus profitable, the treasures in this place can be.
*   **GM Info:** The library is built in stone; a thin layer of dust covers the floor and walls; the ash hill atop has covered the windows and the whole building is dark; air is smoky & senses relying on smell are hindered.

**Room/Encounter Notes:**
*   **Kalankantar the lich-mimic:** Lives in this gigantic study (top right). He is obsessed with books and will let the players go only if they leave all their books & scrolls to him. Else he is a hard encounter. He guards mighty loot.
*   **Archives (D20 Table - point A):**
    *   1-7. Archives
    *   8-9. Dust Mephits
    *   10-11. Smoke Mephits
    *   12-17. Chest, containing 2D6 GP worth of tomes
    *   18-20. Mimic
*   **Three friendly gargoyle heads:** Beg to be moved out of the dungeon, they tell stories of ages past. (Located in the corridor near the staff quarters).
*   **Staff quarters:** Magic boots were lost in this one.
*   **Ink pools:** Aggressive ink jellies live in there (watch out for blinding attacks).
*   **Hall of tapestries:** Only staff allowed at this point. Characters of the tapestries in this hall come alive and fight intruders.
*   **The floor/pit:** The floor collapsed by 35 feet. Staff only put an illusory floor to hide it. Dangerous!
*   **Scriptorium:** This room is a mess. Not touching anything requires a hard dexterity check. If you touch anything, animated quills behave as "magic missiles."
*   **Shrine to the god of literacy:** Big statue of a candle. Lighting it opens and reveals the green secret doors.
*   **Secret room:** Behind a shelf (medium check to spot). Sour black beverage is stored here. It tastes terrible cold but gives a moderate bonus to wisdom & intelligence skills for 15 minutes.
*   **Origami golems:** Wander near the shelves of scrolls. If defeated they reform after 15 minutes.
*   **Librarian's office:** Contains scrolls stored in alphabetical order. 
*   **Key/Loot details:** 
    *   The key to the blue gate has fallen on the floor. It was obviously hidden in a scroll entitled "Entrance (the)".
    *   The yellow key is hidden in a scroll named "Gallery (the)".
    *   There's 5GP in a scroll entitled "Gold," "Loot," or whatever the players can think of.
    *   "Here are also books dedicated to Deneir and/or literacy gods; it is written their symbol is a lighted candle (medium Religion check = the character knows without need to look it up)."

### 3. Map Description (Accessibility Guide)

The map is a top-down, interconnected labyrinth. The scale is marked with a 10-foot bar.

**The Entrance:**
*   You start at the bottom left, labeled "Enter Here." You walk into a room called the **Librarian's Office**. From here, you move right into the **Shelves of Scrolls** area.

**The Central Spine:**
*   From the shelves, you move north into the **Hall of Tapestries**. This is a dangerous central junction. Note: A large portion of the floor here is an illusory trap; the actual floor has collapsed 35 feet.
*   To the west of the hall are the **Staff Quarters** and a side chamber containing **Ink Pools**.
*   To the east of the hall is the **Scriptorium**.

**The Eastern/Southern Wing:**
*   From the Scriptorium, you can head south to the **Shrine to Deneir**. 
*   Behind the shrine is a **Secret Room** (marked with an 'S'), which can be accessed by lighting the candle statue in the shrine.
*   Connecting to the Scriptorium is the **Gallery**, which holds the yellow key.

**The Northern Labyrinth:**
*   From the central Hall of Tapestries, you head north into a large, dense maze of corridors known as the **Fire-proof Caves of Archives**. 
*   This maze occupies the entire top-left and top-center of the map. As you explore these corridors, you roll a D20 on the "Archives" table (marked with an 'A') to determine the contents of each room.

**The Boss Chamber:**
*   In the far top-right corner of the map, isolated from the rest of the layout, is a large, rectangular room: **The Gigantic Study**. This is the lair of Kalankantar the lich-mimic.

**Summary for Play:**
The dungeon funnels you through the central "Hall of Tapestries." Navigating the maze in the north is where the primary combat/loot encounters (Archives) are located. The south-eastern section (Scriptorium, Shrine, Gallery) contains the puzzle elements and the keys needed to unlock the path forward.
