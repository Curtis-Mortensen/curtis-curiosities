### 3. Map Description for Gameplay
To visualize this dungeon, imagine a floating island shaped like a massive pineapple, suspended in a cosmic void. It is roughly 13 levels/areas deep, arranged vertically.

*   **The Approach (Top):** You arrive via **Area 0**, a portal hovering just above the pineapple’s leafy crown. You must drop down onto **Area 1**, the wide landing platform on the roof of the structure.
*   **The Upper "Fruit" (Floors 2–5):** Entering the top of the fruit, you find **Area 2** (the Inn) and **Area 3** (the guest rooms). Deeper in the core of this section is **Area 4** (Admin Control), where a massive Golem resides, and **Area 5** (the bar, "Soylent Love").
*   **The Mid-Section (Floors 6–9):** Moving downward, the architecture becomes more industrial. You encounter **Area 6** (Cryo Facility) and **Area 7** (Observation Lounge), which overlooks the void. This leads into the darker, more restricted **Area 8** (The Warehouse) and **Area 9** (Cargo Bay), where robotic golems handle waste shipments.
*   **The Base/Roots (Floors 10–13):** The bottom of the pineapple contains the agricultural and utility sectors: **Area 10** (Failed Agriponics) and **Area 11** (Agriponics Tourism). Tucked into the side is **Area 12** (a small village/residence), and at the very bottom is **Area 13** (the Portal Gun room).
*   **Hidden Area:** **Area 42** is located somewhere near the geometric center of the pineapple, accessible by navigating the confusing, non-Euclidean hallways that connect the other levels.

**Movement Note:** Because the station is "non-Euclidean," rooms are not connected in a straight line. Navigating between two points requires physical effort (Strength) or mental pattern recognition (Intelligence). Failing these checks causes you to get lost for an extra week.
