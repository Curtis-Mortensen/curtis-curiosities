### Part 1: Transcription of Written Text

#### Intro & Wandering Monsters
**Grime Dungeon by Alex Welk**
*The dungeon’s squatters fear the powerful ooze of the failed Dracolich Klithmnora, the Weeping Knight. Make it a running fight and let her own former lair and traps be her undead undoing. What treasures remain after her bid for immortality?*

**Wandering Monsters (1-in-6 chance every ten minutes in a previously cleared room):**
1. **Skeletal Cadre:** 3 HD (30 HP), AC 10, Med, 30ft move. ATK: Bone Phalanx. *Creatures within 5 feet take damage.*
2. **Slimeoba:** 3 HD (21 HP), AC 9, Small, 10ft move. ATK: +5 Slam (1d6 Blunt + 1d6 Acid). *Slam initiates a grapple (+5). Grappled enemies take 1d6 acid damage on the Slimeoba’s turn.*
3. **1d3+1 Drow Penitent**
4. **Random Trap:** Reactivates in an old space. Roll 1d8 for type.
5. **Spooky sounds or mysterious happening.**
6. **Friendly or Hungry rats** moving into the cleared room.

#### Room Descriptions
1. **Entrance:** Tapestry-covered hall with old, dead garden pits flanking the entrance stairs. A locked stone doorway leads east behind a frayed tapestry. The door south is held by a difficult lock. The door west is badly stuck.
2. **Hallways:** Clean, old stone tiles. Halls are 10ft tall, rooms are 30ft tall, doors are wood with metal banding. Traps wait to be tripped by a pressure plate, hidden tripwire, or magical proximity detector to hit a 20ft length of corridor. Damage is halved on a save. Traps reset in 1d4+2 rounds.
3. **Skeletal Horde:** Faking containment for a surprise attack. Large jail is unlocked. Rotating wall secret passage to room 3 has one-way glass. Store room doors are locked with a simple lock.
4. **Storage:** Rotting storeroom looted for usable lumber. Old food and spell components cross-contaminate. A crawling passage is hidden behind barrels.
5. **Ratfolk Nests:** Former dormitories turned into nests by Ushuk, Shann, Teuaka, Darrow, and Gorn. They seek man-flesh and marrow. Want to remove the devil for its spell circle components. Will attack unless intimidated or made a good offer. Have a map of corridor traps. Don't like the taste of elves.
6. **Ritual Room:** A stylish devil, Zyminalixasix, waits inside a magic circle. Zym will bargain for someone to break the circle. He will twist the deal to backfire once free. He tricked the rats to think his true name has power, fooling them into thinking man-flesh holds strength. His advice on attaining lichdom was seeded with offhand comments about the dragon risking her acidic nature.
7. **Cathedral:** Sooty, red-robed figures (seven Drow worshippers of the sun) chant in the corner of an ancient cathedral. They ignore/resist outsiders. They are trying to contact a sun god to bring light below, but their ideas are incorrect. Storage closet holds a passage hidden behind boxes. South door has a strong lock.
8. **Training Room:** Empty room with training dummies, weapons, straw mats, and metal stakes.
9. **Art Gallery:** Features sad maidens, queens, and women in a romance style. Suffers from poor maintenance and acid burns. Klithmnora will move through here slowly to avoid the paintings.
10. **Necromancy Foyer:** A stone foyer with magical apparatuses, long since rotted and rusted. Arcana checks reveal these are components for creating a lich, with success giving clues of disastrous overcompensation for acid magic.
11. **Corrosion Cave:** Five corrosion beasts scrounge through rusted equipment. This room is difficult terrain.
12. **Necromancer's Death:** A necromancer was killed by acid. On the 1st round, the room and two adventurer corpses appear to melt. Each round after, make a Wisdom save (DC 12) or be unable to see exits and take 2d6 psychic damage. Adjacent room holds a staircase to another floor.
13. **Treasure Hoard:** The unsquandered remainder of Klithmnora’s hoard (bones, gems, coins). Secret entrance to the north only allows one door open at a time; it allows valuables to enter but not leave.
14. **Swordswoman’s Maze:** A maze of wrought-iron fences used by an air-elemental swordswoman as a battleground.
15. **Klithmnora’s Lair:** A pile of bones is encased in a green jelly that jiggles to life when approached. Klithmnora is a failed Dracolich. She has three layers: an outer mindless acidic jelly, an inner animal-cunning bone swarm, and a protected core of an animated magical skull. Destroying a layer exposes the next.

*   **T2 Crushing Pistons:** Dex (DC 13), 3d10 blunt.
*   **T3 Cold Blast:** Con (DC 13), 4d8 cold.
*   **T4 Fire Jets:** Dex (DC 15), 4d6 fire.
*   **T5 Electric Trap:** Con (DC 13), 3d6 electricity.
*   **T6 Spikes:** Dex (DC 15), 2d12+4 piercing.
*   **T7 Acid Blast:** Dex (DC 15), 4d6 acid.
*   **T8 Force Shards:** Dex (DC 14), 4d4+4 force.
