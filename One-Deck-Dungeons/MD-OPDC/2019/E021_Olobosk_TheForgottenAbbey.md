### Gameplay/Map Guide
To play this dungeon, you do not need a visual map; the deck of cards *becomes* the map.

1.  **The Deck Setup:** You have three distinct "Decks" representing the depth of the dungeon.
    *   **The Courtyards:** Shuffle 3 Spades and 6 Clubs into a pile.
    *   **The Sanctum:** Shuffle 4 Hearts and 4 Diamonds into a pile.
    *   **The Undercroft:** Shuffle 6 Spades and 3 Hearts into a pile.
2.  **Exploration:** Start in the Courtyards. Every time the party enters a new location, draw one card. 
    *   If you draw a Club or Spade, refer to the corresponding number on the table to see what the room looks like and what dangers are present. 
    *   If you draw a Heart or Diamond, it is an encounter or loot. Keep these in your hand.
3.  **The "Blackjack" Mechanic:** The game tracks your "depth" or "stability" using card values. Keep a running sum of all cards laid on the table. If the total value of the cards currently forming the "map" exceeds 21, the Abbey shifts—the architecture changes, the monsters reset, and you must reshuffle the decks and start the dungeon flow over.
4.  **Treasures:** When you draw cards from your hand (collected Hearts/Diamonds), you can find the items listed in the "Treasures Table" at the bottom of the page, which provide various magical effects to help the party survive the haunted ruins.
