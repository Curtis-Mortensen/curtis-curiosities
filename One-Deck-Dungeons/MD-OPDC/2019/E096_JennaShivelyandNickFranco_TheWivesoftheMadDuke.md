### Transcription of Text

**Title:** The Wives of the Mad Duke
**By:** Jenna Shively & Nick Franco

**Dream Hook:** A ghostly woman appears to you in a dream: "The mad duke has locked away his pregnant wife. He craves a son. Tomorrow, she will give birth to a daughter. In his rage, he will murder his wife and daughter, just as he has done three times before. Please, save her from our fate! You can find them in the tower. I will help you if I can, but be warned: the spirits of the other queens are restless and unpredictable. They may not wish you to succeed."

**Castle Info (Top Right):** The mad duke's castle is on an isolated heap far from any other town or settlement. It is staffed by clockwork servants who will go about their programmed duties unless hindered.

**Room Descriptions:**
1. **The Entrance Hall:** Lined with portraits of the king's family, including his three dead wives: Arabella (hands clasped in prayer), Tara (wearing an emerald necklace), and Stebitha (holding a filigreed dagger).
2. **Feast Hall:** Contains a dozen tables, a raised dais with two thrones, and the king's sad parrot in a gilded cage. Two sets of stairs lead to the second floor, flanked by 2 clockwork guards. If the players tell the parrot a joke, he reveals secret stairs behind the dais.
3. **Chapel:** The ghost of Agatha prays here. If players join her in prayer, she gives them a holy relic (hidden in the floor) that grants +1 to defense.
4. **Crypt:** Holds the tombs of the dead wives and the empty tomb of the duhe (duke), adorned with a half-finished sculpture holding the duhe's ancestral sword. The sword is enchanted and can provide information if players are stuck.
5. **Kitchens:** A clockwork cook prepares a meal. It will attack if it sees the party. The cupboards contain 2 bottles of fine wine (20g each) and a healing potion.
6. **Greenhouse:** Contains 1 carnivorous plant and 3 poison plants. There are 4 rare plants (worth 5g each). A clockwork gardener, enveloped by vines, holds a jar of "Mir-a-Grow." Applying this to plants will cause them to burst through the roof, creating a path to the second floor.
7. **Library:** Supernaturally flooded (7 feet deep). The door is blocked by floating wreckage. Tara (the ghost) is weeping and ignoring players. 2 sharks circle the room. The emerald necklace is in one of the shark's mouths; returning it to Tara drains the water. Her tears act as a healing potion.
8. **Trophy Room:** Stebitha possesses taxidermied trophies (boar’s head, grizzly bear, suit of armor). Destroying the objects renders her harmless, but using the filigreed dagger found in the room to finish her off grants +1 to attack when fighting the duke.
9. **Tower Transition:** Players hear the wail of an infant and a midwife saying, "It... a girl." The duke howls in rage.
10. **The Tower:** The duke, duchess, and midwife are here. The duke attacks the party, targeting the player with the lowest health. He fights with a twin to the ancestral sword; wielders of the two swords have a 50% miss chance against each other. He summons 2 clockwork guards to assist him. If defeated, the duchess and the ghosts of the three wives vanish.
