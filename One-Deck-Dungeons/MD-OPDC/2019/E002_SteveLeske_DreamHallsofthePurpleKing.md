### Map Description for Play
The dungeon is mapped onto a giant side-profile of a man’s face. 

*   **Entrance:** Players enter at the bottom right corner of the map. This leads to a small, isolated chamber, then through a "trapped BLACK door" into a T-shaped hallway.
*   **The Hub:** The T-shaped hallway connects to a central block of four interconnected rooms arranged in a 2x2 grid:
    *   **Top-Left:** White Room (contains a red squid-thing).
    *   **Top-Right:** Green Room.
    *   **Bottom-Left:** Red Room (contains white spiders).
    *   **Bottom-Right:** Purple Room (contains loot).
*   **The Outer Caverns:** Paths lead outward from the central grid to the edges of the "face":
    *   **The Crown:** A large cave area at the top of the head features a Yellow Ooze Pit and Yellow Spiders. There is also a trapped chest here.
    *   **The Back/Neck:** A large cave on the right contains the 50-foot deep Purple Ooze pit.
    *   **The Nose/Face:** A cavern extending from the nose/mouth area features the Red Ooze pit.
    *   **The Throat/Under-Chin:** A path leads under the chin to the Green Ooze pit and the skeletons.
    *   **The Base:** The bottom of the map consists of a large, jagged pit filled with White Ooze. The gargantuan White Spider roams this lowest area.

The layout is non-linear, allowing players to move from the central hub into the various "features" of the head (the pits and traps) and then back into the hub or deeper into other zones.
