### Map Description for Gameplay
If you were running this for a player who cannot see the map, you can describe it as follows:

**The Setting:**
The map depicts a massive, craggy mountain range painted in deep blues and greys. Three distinct, sharp peaks rise into the air. Above them, a bright yellow sun sits in a dark blue sky.

**The Layout:**
*   **Entry Point:** Adventurers start by scaling the high, precarious peaks. The climb is dangerous—a fall would be catastrophic.
*   **The Upper Levels:** Inside the mountain, accessible from the peaks, is the **Kitchen**. It is sparsely furnished with a table featuring a mysterious, forever-wilted rose. A loose floorboard here acts as a hidden passage downward. Below this is the **Bedroom of the sorcerer**.
*   **The Lair:** At the base of the central mountain sits the **Castle**. Inside is a throne room littered with bones, a staff, and a crown. The central threat is a figure draped over the throne, deceptively still, waiting for the right moment to "POP UP."
*   **The Right Flank:** Located on the lower eastern side of the mountain is a **Statue** of two figures holding a ruby. It acts as a lethal trap: a blue "laser" dot projects onto a riddle inscribed on the wall. Solving it yields the gem; failing results in the walls closing in to crush the party. Nearby, an **Outhouse** stands, and a captive is found pinned by a **web spell**—this NPC is the party’s best source of information regarding the city’s history and the location of hidden treasure.
*   **The Left Flank:** A second **Outhouse** is positioned on the lower western side of the mountain base.

**DM Tip:** The geography is vertical. Players move from the sky-high peaks, down through the mountain's internal chambers (kitchen/bedroom), and end up at the central castle base, with the traps and NPCs positioned along the lower perimeter.
