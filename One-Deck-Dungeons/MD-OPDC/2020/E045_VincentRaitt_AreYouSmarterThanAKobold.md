### Map Description for Navigation
The dungeon is represented on a square grid. Imagine the layout as a series of stone-hewn chambers connected by narrow hallways.

*   **The Exterior:** On the far left of the map, there is a grassy area with trees. There are two entrance points: a main "barred door" and a hidden cave entrance located slightly north of it, which leads into Room 1.
*   **The Entryway (Room 1):** A rectangular room directly inside the entrance. Exiting this room to the east leads to a long horizontal hallway.
*   **The Central Nexus:** Near the middle of the dungeon is a "barred door" icon. This acts as a central hub. 
    *   **North** of this hub leads to a vertical hallway with a spike pit (Trap B) and into **Room 2** (the dormitory).
    *   **East** of the hub leads into a long corridor with a poison gas trap (Trap C) and a poison dart trap (Trap D), which connects to **Room 3** (the chancel).
*   **The East Wing:** From Room 3, a corridor heads south. It contains a spike pit (Trap F) and a flamethrower trap (Trap G). This path leads to **Room 4** (the coffins) and continues further south into **Room 5** (the cafeteria).
*   **The South/West Sector:** 
    *   **Room 6** is located in the bottom-middle of the map, accessible via a corridor heading south from the main hub. This corridor contains a bladed axe trap (Trap J).
    *   **Room 7** (the throne room) is located in the bottom-left corner of the dungeon, connected to the corridor leading to Room 6. 
    *   **Room 8** (the bedroom) is located in the middle-left, tucked between the exterior wall and the central hub, accessible by a small side hallway.
*   **Key Landmarks:** 
    *   **Trap H** acts as a bottleneck in the hallway connecting the cafeteria (Room 5) to the rest of the map.
    *   **Trap I** is located within the cafeteria (Room 5) itself.
    *   **Trap K** is located on the floor of the throne room (Room 7), right in front of the throne.
