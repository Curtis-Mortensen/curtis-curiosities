### 2. Full Text Transcription

#### **Introduction**
Starting when the adventurers enter through the iron doors, the **Dungeon Heart** initiates its **Plan**, summoning **Minions** as listed below, multiplied by the number of adventurers. Summoned Minions move towards the Dungeon Heart and any adventurers inside along an invisible path (purple) in single-file and unaffected by obstructions. If their attack wouldn’t reach an adventurer, they move a 2nd time instead. They ignore any ghostly Heroes. Minions on the map attack adventurers they see, pursuing until victory or death.

The adventurers will need help to not be overrun, and spirits of long-lost Heroes fill this place. Luckily, those Heroes can be empowered to help the adventurers fight off the endless Minions closing in behind them.

#### **Minions**
*   **Mook:** Shadowy, hunched humanoid. 10 HP, M, 13 AC, 25 ft. MOV, +3 Claw (1d8). *Horde: +1 per Mook attacking the same target.*
*   **Spike:** Inky, four-legged beast with shifting spikes. 7 HP, S, 14 AC, 35 ft. MOV, +5 Bite (1d8+3) or +6 Spike Volley [30 ft.] (1d4+4).
*   **Brute:** Lumbering soot giant. 30 HP, L, 16 AC, 25 ft. MOV, +6 Slam (2d6+4). *Shoulder Through: No 2nd move, halve the 1st damage taken each round.*
*   **Crawler:** Lopsided centipede made of ichor and insect corpses. 10 HP per PC (min. 50), H (10 ft. wide, 60 ft. long), 18 AC, 25 ft. MOV. *Sheltering Embrace: All Minions move freely underneath it, gaining +2 AC and take half damage while there. Legs, Uncountable: 3d6 damage to all enemies in Crawler’s space while it is moving.*
*   **Hunger:** Six-legged beast of pure darkness with two heads. 15 HP per PC (min. 75), H, 17 AC, 40 ft. MOV, +8 Bite x2 (2d6+5). *Dark Penumbra: 50% the 1st hit taken each round does no damage.*

#### **Dungeon Heart & Plan**
*   **Dungeon Heart:** Immobile, slimy, black quartz with oozing, shadowy shields. 50 HP per PC (min. 200), L, 10 AC. Regeneration 5, slowly consumes all corpses and treasure left on the floor. Starts using **Core Magic** once the adventurers enter the Heart Chamber.
    *   **Core Magic:** Cast up to one of the following after each adventurer’s turn. All spells are recharged every 5 adventurer turns.
        *   **Minion Rage:** Advantage to all Minion attacks and damage on their next turn. Advance the Plan one turn.
        *   **Dark Invigoration:** All Minions regain 10 HP and make a half move.
        *   **Conjure Stalagmite:** One target, 4d6 damage, DC 15 save for half.
        *   **Barbed Barrier:** Attackers have a 50% chance of missing the Heart unless they take 1d6 damage. Lasts 1 round.

**Dungeon Heart Plan Table**
*   1-9: Nothing. The Dungeon Heart wants its victims lured inside...
*   10: A portal opens at the Entry. Mook
*   11: Mook. “Welcome to my domain, mortals...”
*   12: Mook x2
*   13: Nothing. “...drop your most costly item and you may leave.” [True. Extortion is safer than a fight. Minions won’t attack while leaving.]
*   14: Spike | 15: Mook | 16: Mook | 17: Brute
*   18: Nothing. “You merely banish my Minions temporarily.”
*   19: Crawler | 20: Mook x2 | 21: Mook x2, Spike x2 | 22: Mook x2, Brute x2 | 23: Brute x3
*   24: Nothing. “Their energy and yours will feed me after you die.”
*   25: Hunger | 26+: Mook, Spike, Brute. Repeat until Dungeon Heart is defeated.

#### **Heroes (Recruitable Spirits)**
A ghostly sage appears in the Art Room to explain the details. She conjures a real-time map showing Heroes and Minions. Each adventurer gains **Renown** for each Minion killed. Spend Renown to recruit Heroes (Costs: 10, 15, 20, 25, 30...). Placed in any explored room; act on recruiter’s turn. 25 MP (Minion Points) restores lost *Skills*.
*   **Marksman:** Fires two arrows, dealing 5 damage each. [Sniper] Arrows deal 10 damage instead.
*   **Swordsman:** Slashes all enemies within 10 ft. for 15 damage. [Champion] Any enemy that comes within 10ft. takes 5 damage.
*   **Warlock:** Lobs a 10 ft. radius blast at any point, dealing 4 damage. [Archmage] Can instead attack in a 120 ft. line, 5 ft. wide.
*   **Priest:** Projects a 30 ft. slow aura. Enemies treat this as difficult terrain. [Paladin] Enemies inside the aura take double damage.
*   **Thief:** Throws a knife for 5 damage within 30 ft. Triple damage if target is below max. [Assassin] After a kill, make another attack, up to twice per turn.

#### **Loot**
*   **Ghost Jacks:** Spectral caltrops; 20 ft. radius sphere of difficult terrain that deals 2d6 damage to intangibles. At Entry.
*   **Pile of 103 high-quality, fake gold coins:** Art Room.
*   **Tiny Mook in a Jar:** Hostile until Heart is defeated, then obedient. Respawns each dawn. False Storage Room.
*   **Dark Essence:** Inhale to cast a weak summon. DC 10 Dex save to catch it before it dissipates. Final Corridor.
*   **Quickpouch:** 6-vial potion belt, use as a thought. Heart Chamber.
*   **Shadow Carapace:** Chitinous skull crown. Reaction to cover 30 ft. area in insects; insects take damage instead of friends. Up to 30 HP/day.
*   **Shade Veins:** Lace cloak of blood vessels. Users share 10 rounds/day of Haste. (Hunger’s corpse).
*   **Dungeon Core Shard:** 12 charges/day to summon Minions (Mook 2, Spike 3, Brute 4). If given to Heroes, it shatters to grant a boon: Summon one *Skilled* Hero per day for 1 hour.

### 3. Map Description
The map is a top-down, interconnected dungeon layout consisting of several distinct chambers connected by hallways, all grid-marked in 5 ft. squares.

*   **Entry:** The map starts at a bottom-left rectangular "Entry Hall." There are decorative pillars flanking the path forward.
*   **The Path:** A purple line runs through the dungeon, representing the "invisible path" that Minions follow.
*   **Trap Room:** Located centrally, north of the entry. It contains "Pit Traps and Swinging Spike Walls."
*   **Art Room:** Located east of the entry. It contains "Empty Frames and Plinths." A ghostly sage appears here.
*   **Training Room:** Located north of the Art Room. It features "Dummies and Targets."
*   **False Storage Room:** Located north of the Trap Room. It contains "Empty Boxes and Locked Chests."
*   **Final Corridor:** A long hallway connecting the storage area to the Heart Chamber. It contains "Difficult Terrain" and a "Gate that takes a turn to open."
*   **Muck Room:** A small chamber east of the Final Corridor.
*   **Heart Chamber:** The final room at the top center of the map. It is characterized by "Oozing Shadows and Ash Piles" and is the location of the **Dungeon Heart**.
