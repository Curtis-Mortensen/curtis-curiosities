**[Introductory Text]**
"The villagers told us where the angel fell, pointing north to the hills. We were generous with royal coin, but none except a drunken sellsword would guide our expedition. Superstitious peasantry. Hiking up, trees lay ruined, flat as by fierce storms, many burnt. Come dusk, some glowed. My soldiers nearly had to use force to pull the sages from curious twittering. Mules and canaries grew ornery. When we reached the scar’s end, the crater was black and smooth, granite splashed like wax. The Object lay half buried where it fell. I mused it seemed a titan’s helm, or the lid of a soup tureen. The men said more an overturned saucer or a snail. The sages blathered about “asymmetrical helix topology”. Surface was silver, iridescent. My palm felt waves of faint buzz, my fingertips slipped from it as if greased. Not even my own blade marks it. Set the men to drag logs for palisade and outbuildings, cut stone for towers, dig latrines and drainage. The sages sketched and whined about tightness of their laboratory and quarters. I expect the entire fort and complex completed by equinox, with the Object sheltered. It is very beautiful. The sages have begun to argue how to prize it open, humming and scribbling. I’m sure the Generals will see value, once we obtain whatever treasures lie within."

**[Room Descriptions]**
*   **Main Entry:** The steel-riveted iron double doors are wrecked. The space is lit by an oil lamp. Seated at a desk is Lieutenant Krupp (Cult Fanatic). He is oblivious to an eyeball parasite on his face. Handout 1 is found here.
*   **Commander’s Private Quarters:** Contains Commander Jeb “the Juggernaut” Happly (Fighter, party level +2). He is suffering from proximity to the alien craft and hallucinates. He wears medals worth 400gp.
*   **Bathrooms:** A large sunken common bath with stagnant water. A Gibbering Mouther hides in the latrines. A hidden panel contains linens and oils worth 400gp.
*   **Barracks:** Bunks and lockers for thirty, plus a weight room. Bulgakov the possessed cosmonaut may be found here. Searching yields 551 sp and a jury-rigged still.
*   **Watchtowers:** Accessed by an iron ladder. A zombie guard tolls the alarm. A plasma gun (lightning bolt effect, low battery) is located here.
*   **Observatory:** The level above the watchtower houses a massive telescope worth 10,000gp.
*   **Lab:** A mess of metal tables, chalkboards, and tuning forks/tubular bells used to interact with the Object (26 bells, one for each prime number). Searching reveals two scrolls of detect magic. A secret hatch hides an empty box with an "IOU" note.
*   **The Hangar:** Contains the saucer-shaped Object. It is immune to magic and physical force. It responds to prime-numbered tone combinations (2+19+79, 2+31+67, or 2+37+61) to open. Inside is a core chamber with a Pilot (Aboleth) and a glowing Orb that deals 2d6 damage per strike.

### Map Description for Non-Visual Play
The dungeon is an enclosed fortification built around a central, circular crater.

*   **Central Feature:** The **Hangar (A)** is the heart of the site. It is a large circular room. In the very center lies the **"Object,"** a large, spiral-shaped, saucer-like craft half-buried in the ground at a 20-degree angle.
*   **Surrounding Structure:** A thick, ring-shaped perimeter wall surrounds the Hangar. There is a narrow corridor running inside this wall. 
*   **The Rooms:** Several rooms are built into the perimeter structure, accessible via the corridor or internal doors:
    *   To the **North** is the **Main Entrance (H)**.
    *   To the **East** is the **Lab (E)**.
    *   To the **South** are the **Barracks (B)** and **Commander's Quarters (C)**.
    *   To the **West** are the **Bathrooms (D)** and **Holding Cells (F)**.
*   **Watchtowers (G):** Two watchtowers are positioned at the extreme northwest and northeast corners of the outer walls, accessible by internal ladders.
*   **Burnt Grounds (I):** The area immediately surrounding the exterior of the fort is scorched and cratered, indicating the point of impact.

**Spatial Logic:** Think of the map as a target. The Object is the bullseye. The Hangar is the inner ring. The rooms (A-F) form the outer ring, and the exterior (I) is the surrounding wilderness. You move from the exterior into the Main Entrance, which leads into the corridor loop, providing access to all individual rooms before entering the central Hangar.
