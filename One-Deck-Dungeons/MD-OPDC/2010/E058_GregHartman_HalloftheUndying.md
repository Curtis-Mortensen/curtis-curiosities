**Header**
Hall of the Undying — A One-Page Dungeon by Greg Hartman

**Background**
Ages ago, a necromancer, aspiring to be as the necromancer-kings of old, built this tomb in a hidden place, accessible only through a magical portal. He meant for this place to be his starting point for domination of his home nation, and filled its halls with a twisted mockery of life and comfort to entertain his apprentices.

**Room Descriptions**
1. **The portal:** The PCs came in through a 7' diameter circle of swirling shadows on the NW wall.
2. **Sitting room:** Well-appointed with overstuffed chairs, small tables, and candelabras. Shelves hold books of gruesome tales. A bar on the north wall has liquor/cups (100 gp value). The south wall features a puppet stage with spectral marionettes.
3. **Scrying room:** Contains mirrors, orbs, and basins. A skull on a side-table speaks and answers questions; it is poorly informed and slightly crazy. If accosted, it attacks by biting.
4. **Guardroom:** Locked. Contains 3 ghouls and 1 wight in stasis, released if entered without the key from area 17. Contains rusted weapons.
5. **Necromancer's vault:** Contains a table, chair, candelabrum, and two coffers of treasure (3,000 sp, 1,000 gp, jewelry, and gems).
6. **Hidden vault entrance:** A quasit familiar guards this area, often disguised as a shadowy bat-thing (80% hidden chance). The northern secret door is trapped; opening it triggers a 20' pit filled with 10d3 crawling claws.
7. **The hidden vault:** Contains a trapped chest (poison needle) holding the necromancer’s coded spellbook and various magic items (ring of protection +2, potions, staves, wands, cloak, and dagger).
8. **Library:** Contains four spellbooks (various levels), workbooks on creating magic/undead, and two shadows hiding in the shelves.
9. **Morgue:** Smells of embalming spices. Contains a lab bench and four steel slabs holding three juju zombies and one mummy.
10. **Ossuary:** Walls and chandeliers made of bone. Floor is stone. Getting too close to walls causes them to grab (save at -4, 1d6 dmg/round). Attacking walls/bones releases 2d8 skeletons.
11. **Cells:** Small, dismal cells with a bucket and straw mattress.
12. **Torture room:** Contains a rack and table. Two insane ghast torturers attack immediately. Spending >10 rounds here causes victims to hear wailing (fear spell save).
13. **Dining hall:** Long table with tarnished silver service. Macabre paintings and tapestries. Eight zombie servants wait here.
14. **Kitchen:** Four juju zombies dressed as chefs. Contains rotting food.
15. **Pantry:** Kept magically cold. Contains rotten meat/dairy. Examining closely requires a save or be sickened for 2d4 rounds.
16. **Summoning circle:** Contains a trapped erinyes who will bargain for her release.
17. **Tomb:** Contains a sarcophagus with the necromancer’s rotted corpse. The pockets contain the keys to areas 4 and 6. A pedestal holds a fist-sized diamond containing the necromancer’s soul. He will promise anything to be freed.
