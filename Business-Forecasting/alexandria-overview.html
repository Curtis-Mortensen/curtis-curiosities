Investor Brief · July 2026

Alexandria

Narrator-driven D&D-style one-shots on Discord — where a rules engine keeps the math honest and AI voices tell the story.

“A narrator-driven one-shot where the computer is the rules referee and the voices are the storytellers.”

Model Subscription SaaS Platform Discord-native Stage Pre-revenue · MVP in build Founder Curtis Mortensen

01 · Market

Target Audience

Friend groups who already play tabletop RPGs — especially D&D — and already hang out on Discord. Alexandria is not replacing campaign DMs. It fills the empty chair on game night.

  • 3–4 players in voice chat, one evening session
  • Groups that cancel when nobody can prep or run a session
  • Players who want real dice math, not AI-hallucinated rolls
  • Discord-native — no new app to install for game night

02 · Product

MVP Functionality

Self-running one-shot adventures: join Discord, pick characters, play a prepared story with voiced narration.

Rules Engine Dice, HP, combat, valid moves — enforced in code
+
Narrator Voices Bard, Challenger & more — scene prose & roleplay
  • Featured adventure: Temple of the Moon Priests (3–5 hr one-shot)
  • Adventure Lite & D&D 5e rules editions
  • Text + voice-message input; TTS narration in Discord voice
  • Operator review mode for live-session quality control
